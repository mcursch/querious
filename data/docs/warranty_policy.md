# Warranty Policy

*Effective January 1, 2025. Applies to all products sold by Acme Outfitters.*

## Overview

Acme Outfitters stands behind the quality of everything we sell. We offer a limited warranty against manufacturing defects for all product categories. Warranty periods and coverage vary by category. This policy covers products purchased directly from Acme Outfitters (acmeoutfitters.example, our retail stores, or authorized B2B accounts). Products purchased from unauthorized third-party resellers are not covered by this warranty.

---

## Warranty Coverage by Category

### Tents and Shelters

**Warranty period:** Lifetime on poles and structural seams; 3 years on fabric and coatings; 1 year on zippers, buckles, and hardware.

Coverage includes: pole fractures under normal use, seam delamination, factory-defective zipper sliders, waterproof coating failure before the rated lifespan.

Not covered: UV degradation after expected lifespan, tears from contact with sharp objects, damage from improper storage (wet packing), rodent damage, flood damage, or use beyond the tent's rated capacity.

### Backpacks and Packs

**Warranty period:** Lifetime on frame, main body fabric, and load-bearing seams; 3 years on harness and hip-belt padding; 1 year on buckles, zippers, and compression straps.

Coverage includes: frame cracks under normal load (within the pack's rated capacity), harness delamination, load-bearing seam failures, broken plastic buckles.

Not covered: cosmetic abrasion, torn mesh pockets from overstuffing, shoulder-strap wear from normal use after 3 years, damage caused by airline baggage handling without a pack cover or travel bag.

### Stoves and Cookware

**Warranty period:** 5 years on stove bodies and valves; 1 year on igniters and O-rings; 90 days on cookware nonstick coatings.

Coverage includes: valve failure preventing gas flow, stove-body cracking under normal use, igniter failure within warranty period.

Not covered: igniter failure caused by water ingress, damage from dropping on hard surfaces, degraded nonstick coating from metal utensil use, damage from cleaning with abrasive scourers.

### Apparel

**Warranty period:** 1 year from purchase date.

Coverage includes: seam failures, zipper failure on outerwear, delamination of waterproof-breathable membranes within the rated lifespan (typically 2–3 seasons depending on care).

Not covered: pilling from normal use, fading, shrinkage from improper laundering, tears, or damage from improper storage.

### Climbing Hardware (Carabiners, Quickdraws, Cams)

**Warranty period:** 3 years on gate mechanisms and spine integrity; 1 year on slings and cord.

**Critical safety note:** Any piece of climbing hardware that has been subjected to a fall greater than 1 kN, has visible nicks or gouges on the gate or spine, or has been exposed to chemicals must be retired immediately regardless of warranty status. Warranty coverage does not extend the safe service life of personal protective equipment.

Coverage includes: gate stiffness or failure to close/lock on first use or within the first year, carabiner markings not matching rated specifications.

Not covered: wear from rope or rock contact, gate damage from rock impact, UV degradation of slings.

### Footwear

**Warranty period:** 1 year from purchase date.

Coverage includes: midsole delamination, outsole separation, hardware failure (lace hooks, speed laces, BOA dials), waterproof membrane failure before end of rated life.

Not covered: outsole wear (normal wear rate depends on terrain and mileage), upper abrasion, lace breakage, odor.

### Accessories (Trekking Poles, Headlamps, Hydration Systems)

**Warranty period:** 2 years on structural components; 1 year on electronics and batteries; 90 days on hydration bladder welds and bite valves.

## Warranty Claim Process

### Step 1 — Check Eligibility

Review the coverage details above. The item must have been purchased within the warranty period and from an authorized Acme Outfitters channel. Have your order number or receipt ready.

### Step 2 — Contact Support

Email warranty@acmeoutfitters.example or call 1-800-226-3348 (Mon–Fri 8 AM – 6 PM PT). Include:
- Order number or proof of purchase
- Product name and SKU (found on the tag or original packaging)
- A clear description of the defect
- Photos showing the defect (required for claims over $100)

### Step 3 — Ship the Item (if required)

For most claims, we will send a prepaid return label. For items under $30, we may issue a replacement without requesting the defective item back. For large items (tents, frame packs), we will provide a freight label.

### Step 4 — Resolution

Once the item is received and inspected (typically within 5–7 business days), we will:
1. Repair the item if a repair is feasible and the item is otherwise in good condition.
2. Replace the item with the same model or the nearest current equivalent if it is discontinued.
3. Issue a refund to the original payment method if neither repair nor replacement is available.

We aim to resolve all warranty claims within **14 business days** of receiving the returned item.

## Exclusions and Limitations

This warranty does not cover:
- Damage caused by accident, misuse, neglect, unauthorized modification, or failure to follow care instructions
- Normal wear and tear
- Products damaged by acts of nature (flood, fire, earthquake)
- Second-hand items or items purchased from unauthorized resellers
- Items used commercially (guiding, rental programs) unless covered by an Enterprise warranty agreement

This limited warranty gives you specific legal rights; you may have additional rights under applicable state or local law. Some states do not allow limitations on implied warranties, so some of the above exclusions may not apply to you.
