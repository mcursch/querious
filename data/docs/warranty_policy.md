# Warranty Policy

## Overview

Acme Outfitters warrants its products against defects in materials and workmanship for the
periods described below, when used under normal conditions and maintained according to our care
instructions. This warranty covers manufacturing defects, not damage from accidents, misuse,
or normal wear and tear.

## What Is Not Covered

This warranty does **not** cover:

- Damage from accidents, falls, or improper use
- Normal wear and tear (fraying, fading, worn-through soles)
- Damage from improper storage or care (mold, UV degradation from extended sun exposure)
- Modifications or repairs performed by anyone other than Acme Outfitters
- Products purchased from unauthorized resellers
- Consumables (fuel, batteries, food cartridges)

## How to File a Warranty Claim

1. Gather proof of purchase (order number or receipt).
2. Take clear photographs of the defect.
3. Email warranty@acmeoutfitters.example with subject line "Warranty Claim — [Order Number]",
   including the photos and a brief description.
4. Our warranty team will respond within 3 business days.
5. If the claim is approved, you will receive a prepaid shipping label to return the item.
6. Upon receipt and inspection, we will repair, replace, or credit the item at our discretion.

## Warranty Remedies

| Outcome | Resolution |
|---|---|
| Repairable defect | Repaired and returned at no charge |
| Replacement available | Replacement unit shipped at no charge |
| Discontinued item | Store credit for current retail value |

We do not offer cash refunds for warranty claims on items outside the standard 30-day return window.

## Lifetime Repair Program

In addition to our warranty, we offer a paid Lifetime Repair Program for all packs and tents.
For a flat fee of $35 plus shipping, we will repair any damage — including non-warranty damage
such as tears, broken buckles, and worn zippers — for the life of the product.
