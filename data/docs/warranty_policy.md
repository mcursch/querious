_Last updated: January 15, 2025_

## 1. Our Warranty Philosophy

Acme Outfitters warrants that every product we manufacture or private-label will be free from
defects in materials and workmanship for the duration stated in this policy. This warranty reflects
our belief that outdoor gear should last, and our commitment to standing behind the products that
carry the Acme Outfitters name.

This policy covers **Acme Outfitters-branded products only**. Third-party brands sold through our
website or catalog are subject to the manufacturer's own warranty; contact that manufacturer
directly for claims.

## 3. What Is Not Covered

The warranty does **not** cover:

1. **Normal wear and tear** — fabric abrasion, fading, seam tape delamination after years of use,
   grip degradation on poles and axes.
2. **Consumable components** — tent pole shock cord, zipper sliders (after manufacturer-defect
   period), stove O-rings and seals, DWR treatments, headlamp batteries, carbide tips.
3. **Accidental damage** — tears, cuts, burns, crush damage, animal chewing or claw damage.
4. **Improper care** — damage resulting from failure to follow care instructions (e.g., machine
   washing a sleeping bag without a front-loader, storing a wet tent without drying).
5. **Modifications** — any alteration, repair attempted by unauthorized parties, or use of
   non-original replacement parts.
6. **Theft or loss.**
7. **Cosmetic damage** — dents, scratches, and discoloration that do not affect function.
8. **Water damage to electronics** beyond the rated ingress protection (IP) rating.
9. **Products purchased from unauthorized resellers.** Acme Outfitters-branded products must be
   purchased through acmeoutfitters.example or an authorized dealer to be eligible.

## 4. Safety-Critical Products (Climbing and Avalanche Equipment)

Climbing harnesses, helmets, ropes, carabiners, and avalanche beacons are safety-critical. In
addition to the standard warranty:

- **Any product involved in a fall or serious load event must be retired and is not eligible for
  warranty repair or exchange** — it must be replaced.
- Warranty period on harnesses and helmets **begins on the manufacture date stamped inside the
  product**, not the purchase date. Check the label before purchase.
- Acme Outfitters will issue a **50 % discount on a replacement** of the same or equivalent model
  when a safety-critical product is retired after a documented fall (proof required: incident
  description and photo of damaged product).

## 5. How to File a Warranty Claim

### Step 1 — Gather Information

Collect:
- Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt) showing the purchase date and the
  authorized retailer.
- Product name and SKU (found on the hang-tag or inside label).
- Clear photos of the defect from multiple angles.
- Brief written description of when and how the defect appeared.

### Step 2 — Submit the Claim

Submit online at **acmeoutfitters.example/warranty** or email
**warranty@acmeoutfitters.example** with the above information. Phone submissions are accepted at
**1-800-555-2267** (Mon–Fri 8 AM–5 PM MT) for customers who cannot submit online.

### Step 3 — Initial Review

Our Warranty team will review photos and respond with one of the following within **5 business
days**:

- **Approved — advance replacement shipped:** For clear-cut defects on in-stock items, we ship a
  replacement before receiving the defective unit. A credit-card hold equal to the product's
  current retail price is placed and released when the defective unit is received.
- **Approved — return first:** A prepaid return shipping label is emailed. Once we receive and
  inspect the item (within 3 business days of receipt), we ship a replacement or issue a refund.
- **Needs physical inspection:** For complex or disputed cases, a prepaid label is issued. Our
  technicians examine the product; a decision is returned within **10 business days** of receipt.
- **Denied:** A written explanation is provided. Denied claims may be appealed within 30 days by
  emailing **warranty@acmeoutfitters.example** with additional evidence.

### Step 4 — Resolution Options

When a warranty claim is approved:

| Option | Details |
|---|---|
| **Replacement** | Same product or current equivalent if original is discontinued. No charge. |
| **Repair** | Acme Outfitters repairs the item at no charge and returns it (if repair is feasible). |
| **Store Credit** | Full retail value as Acme Outfitters store credit (no restocking fee). |
| **Refund** | Available within the first **12 months** of purchase only; refunded to original payment method. |

## 7. Third-Party Brand Warranty Assistance

If you purchased a third-party brand from Acme Outfitters and need warranty assistance, we will:

- Forward your claim to our wholesale rep if the brand is unresponsive (for claims within

limited to repair, replacement, or refund of the defective product. We are not liable for
incidental or consequential damages including, but not limited to, trip cancellation costs,
evacuation fees, or personal injury resulting from product failure.
