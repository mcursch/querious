# Warranty Policy

## Coverage by Category

| Category | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Tents & Shelters | Lifetime limited warranty |
| Backpacks | Lifetime limited warranty |
| Sleeping Bags | 3-year limited warranty |
| Stoves & Cookware | 2-year limited warranty |
| Apparel | 1-year limited warranty |
| Accessories | 1-year limited warranty |

## What Is Covered

- Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
- Hardware failures (buckles, zippers, poles, seams) under normal use

## What Is Not Covered

- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from improper use, storage, or accidents
- Cosmetic damage (fading, minor abrasions)
- Modifications or unauthorized repairs

## Claim Process

Contact warranty@acmeoutfitters.example with proof of purchase, photos, and description of the defect. We will respond within 5 business days.
