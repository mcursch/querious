# Warranty Policy — Acme Outfitters

## Overview

Acme Outfitters warrants all products against defects in materials and workmanship for the periods specified below. This warranty is provided to the original purchaser and is non-transferable. It covers manufacturing defects only; it does not cover damage from normal wear, misuse, accidents, unauthorized modifications, or failure to follow care instructions.

## Warranty Periods by Category

| Product Category | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Tents & Shelters | **2 years** from date of purchase |
| Backpacks & Daypacks | **3 years** from date of purchase |
| Stoves & Cook Systems | **1 year** from date of purchase |
| Climbing Hardware | **2 years** from date of purchase (see note below) |
| Apparel & Footwear | **1 year** from date of purchase |
| Sleeping Bags | **2 years** from date of purchase |
| Accessories & Small Gear | **1 year** from date of purchase |

**Important — Climbing Hardware:** While hardware carries a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects, any climbing protection, carabiner, belay device, or rope that has been subjected to a factor-2 fall, a major impact, or any load exceeding its rated UIAA strength must be retired immediately regardless of visible damage. Warranty claims for climbing hardware that has been loaded in a fall will not be honored; this is a safety classification, not a policy limitation.

## What the Warranty Covers

- Stitching failures and seam separation under normal use loads
- Zipper failures (slider, pull tabs, coil separation) within the warranty period when used as intended
- Frame cracks or weld failures in backpack frames
- Tent pole breakage not caused by improper staking, guying in high winds exceeding the tent's rated conditions, or storage with wet poles
- Coating delamination on rain flies and rainwear
- Burner valve failures on stoves that occur under normal use conditions
- Buckle and hardware failures under normal load

## What the Warranty Does Not Cover

- Damage from misuse, overloading, or use outside the product's intended purpose
- UV degradation of fabrics and coatings (sun exposure over time is considered normal wear)
- Damage from improper washing or care (always follow the care instructions on the product label)
- Damage from animals (rodents, insects, bears)
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, scuffs, color fading) that does not affect functionality
- Lost or stolen items
- Products purchased from unauthorized third-party resellers
- Products with altered, removed, or defaced serial numbers or labels

## How to File a Warranty Claim

1. **Gather your documentation.** You will need your original order number or invoice, the product's SKU (found on the original packaging or a tag on the product), and photos clearly showing the defect.
2. **Submit the claim online** at support.acmeoutfitters.example → "Warranty & Repairs." Describe the defect in detail and upload your photos.
3. **Acme reviews the claim** within 3–5 business days. We may request additional photos or ask you to ship the item for inspection.
4. **Resolution options** — depending on the nature of the defect and product availability, Acme will, at its sole discretion:
   - **Repair** the item (at no charge to you, including return shipping)
   - **Replace** with the same item or the closest current equivalent
   - **Issue a refund** equal to the original purchase price if repair and replacement are not feasible

## Shipping Costs for Warranty Claims

Acme Outfitters covers inbound shipping costs for accepted warranty claims (we provide a prepaid UPS label). If a claim is inspected and found not to be a manufacturing defect, the item will be returned to you at your expense, and we will contact you before returning.

## Extended Warranty / Acme Protection Plan

Acme Outfitters does not currently offer an extended warranty or protection plan beyond the standard periods listed above. This may change in future product releases; check the product page for the most current information.

## Warranty & the Return Policy

The 30-day return policy and the warranty program are separate programs. If a defect is discovered within the 30-day return window, you may choose either path — return for a full refund or file a warranty claim. After 30 days, only the warranty process is available for defects. Change-of-mind returns are not covered by warranty at any time.
