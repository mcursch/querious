# Return and Refund Policy

*Effective March 1, 2025. Applies to all orders placed on or after this date.*

## Overview

Acme Outfitters wants every customer to be satisfied with their purchase. If a product does not meet your expectations, we make it straightforward to return it. This policy describes eligibility, exclusions, timelines, and the mechanics of each return channel.

---

## Return Window

### Standard Return Window

Most items may be returned within **30 days** of the delivery date for a full refund to the original payment method, provided the item is in new, unused condition with original tags and packaging intact.

### Extended Holiday Window

Orders placed between November 15 and December 31 may be returned through January 31 of the following year. This extension applies to gifted items as well; proof of purchase (order number or gift receipt) is required.

### Used-Gear Returns

Items that have been used in the field but are returned due to a **manufacturing defect** are handled under the Warranty Policy (see `warranty_policy.md`), not this return policy. Items that have been used and are being returned for personal preference—fit issues, change of plans—are not eligible for return after 30 days and are not eligible for a refund under any circumstances once used outside normal handling. "Normal handling" means wearing a pack indoors or pitching a tent in your backyard to check fit; it does not mean using gear on a trip.

---

## Exclusions

The following items are **not eligible for return**:

- Personalized or custom-embroidered items
- Items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase
- Opened food, fuel canisters, or chemical fire starters
- Underwear, baselayer bottoms, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Climbing harnesses and helmets that have been used or load-tested
- Gift cards and store credit

Discontinued products sold as clearance may be returned within 14 days of delivery provided they are unused and in original packaging; clearance items are not eligible for the extended holiday window.

---

## How to Initiate a Return

### Online Orders

1. Log in to your Acme Outfitters account at acmeoutfitters.example/account.
2. Select the order and choose **Return Items**.
3. Select the items, choose a reason, and select **Refund** or **Exchange**.
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label (UPS or USPS depending on package weight).
5. Drop off the package within **14 days** of initiating the return request; labels expire after 14 days.

### In-Store Returns

Bring the item and proof of purchase (order confirmation email or packing slip) to any Acme Outfitters retail location. In-store staff can process refunds on the spot for eligible items.

### Enterprise / B2B Returns

Enterprise account holders should contact their account manager to arrange returns. Bulk returns of 10 or more units require an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number, which will be emailed within one business day of request.

## Refund Timelines

| Refund Method | Processing Time |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Original credit/debit card | 3–5 business days after we receive the item |
| ACH / bank transfer | 5–7 business days after we receive the item |
| PayPal | 1–3 business days after we receive the item |
| Store credit (optional) | Instant upon return processing |
| Check (paper, B2B accounts) | 10–14 business days after we receive the item |

Refunds are issued once the returned item has been received and inspected at a distribution center. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is issued.

---

## Restocking Fees

### Standard Restocking Fee

Acme Outfitters does not charge a restocking fee for standard returns within the 30-day window when items are returned in new, unused condition.

### Exceptions

A **15% restocking fee** applies to:
- Items returned in non-original packaging (e.g., box is damaged, tags are removed)
- Tents returned without all poles, stakes, and bags present
- Items returned more than 30 days after delivery (when a late return is accepted at management discretion)

The restocking fee is deducted from the refund amount. If the restocking fee would exceed the refund amount, no cash refund is issued and store credit for the net amount is offered instead.

---

## Exchanges

Exchanges are processed as a return + a new order. When you select **Exchange** during the return flow, we place a new order for the replacement item and ship it as soon as we confirm the return label was scanned by the carrier. The replacement order is charged to your original payment method; the refund from the returned item offsets the charge within 5 business days.

---

## Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive an item that is damaged in transit or is not what you ordered, contact support@acmeoutfitters.example within **7 days** of delivery. Include your order number and photos of the damage or incorrect item. We will send a prepaid return label and ship the correct or replacement item at no additional charge, prioritizing expedited delivery.
