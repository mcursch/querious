# Return & Refund Policy

## Overview

Acme Outfitters stands behind every product we sell. If you are not completely satisfied with your
purchase, we offer a straightforward return and refund process.

## Return Window

- **Standard items:** 30 days from the date of delivery.
- **Holiday purchases:** Items purchased between November 15 and December 31 may be returned through
  January 31 of the following year.
- **Defective items:** Contact support within 90 days of delivery. Defective items are eligible for
  full refund or replacement regardless of the standard window.

## Eligible Items

Most items in new, unused condition with original tags and packaging are eligible for return.

### Non-Returnable Items

The following items **cannot be returned**:

- Items that have been used, washed, or worn (other than trying on for fit)
- Customized or monogrammed items
- Clearance/final-sale items marked "No Returns"
- Consumables (fuel canisters, food, first-aid supplies once opened)
- Climbing hardware (harnesses, carabiners, ropes) that have been loaded or show signs of use —
  safety equipment cannot be resold
- Gift cards

## Restocking Fees

| Condition | Restocking Fee |
|---|---|
| New, original packaging | None |
| New, missing original packaging | 10% of purchase price |
| Opened but unused (e.g., assembled tent returned same day) | 15% of purchase price |

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to your account at acmeoutfitters.example/account.
2. Select the order and the item(s) you wish to return.
3. Choose a reason and select your preferred resolution: **refund** or **store credit** (+10% bonus
   on store credit value).
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label.
5. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any UPS location.

Enterprise customers with a dedicated account manager may initiate returns through their
account manager directly.

## Refund Timeline

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit / Debit Card | 5–10 business days after we receive the return |
| ACH / Bank Transfer | 3–7 business days after we receive the return |
| PayPal | 3–5 business days after we receive the return |
| Check | 10–15 business days (check mailed upon return receipt) |
| Store Credit | Applied immediately upon return receipt |

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. We do not issue cash refunds.

## Exchanges

Direct exchanges are available for size/color variants of the same product at no extra charge,
subject to availability. Exchanges ship free via standard ground shipping.

## International Returns

International customers (outside the US) are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs
duties. Refunds will not include original shipping charges.

## Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact support@acmeoutfitters.example within 7 days of
delivery. We will arrange return shipping at our cost and prioritize a replacement or refund.
