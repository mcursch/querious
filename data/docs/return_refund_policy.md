# Return & Refund Policy

## Overview

Acme Outfitters stands behind the quality of every product we sell. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

## Return Window

All unused, unaltered merchandise may be returned within **30 days** of the original purchase date for a full refund.

## Condition Requirements

Items must be returned in their original condition:

- **New/unused condition**: Tags attached, original packaging intact.
- **Used/opened items**: We do **not** accept returns on items that have been used in the field, washed, or altered, with the following exceptions:
  - Defective merchandise (see Warranty Policy)
  - Items that arrived damaged due to shipping

**Used tents, sleeping bags, and apparel are not eligible for return** unless they are defective. "Used" means the item has been taken outdoors, set up, slept in, or worn in any conditions.

## Exclusions

The following items are **non-returnable**:

- Used or field-tested gear
- Customized or engraved products
- Fuel canisters (opened)
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Gift cards

## Refund Timelines

| Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit card | 5–7 business days after receipt |
| ACH / bank transfer | 7–10 business days after receipt |
| Check | 10–14 business days after receipt |
| PayPal | 3–5 business days after receipt |
| Store credit | Immediate upon approval |

## Restocking Fees

A **15% restocking fee** applies to:

- Orders over $500 returned in original, unopened condition
- Special-order items not part of regular inventory
- Returned sets or bundles where individual pieces are missing

No restocking fee applies to defective items or returns made within 7 days of purchase.

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Contact our support team at returns@acmeoutfitters.example or open a support ticket.
2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
3. We will issue a **Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA)** number within 1–2 business days.
4. Ship the item prepaid to the address on your RMA confirmation.
5. Refund is issued within the timeline above after we receive and inspect the item.

## Exchanges

We process exchanges as a return + new order. Exchanges follow the same eligibility rules as returns.

## International Returns

Customers outside the continental US are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs duties. Refund amounts exclude original shipping charges.
