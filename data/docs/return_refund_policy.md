# Return & Refund Policy — Acme Outfitters

## Overview

Acme Outfitters stands behind every product we sell. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we make the return process as straightforward as possible. Please read this policy carefully before initiating a return, as different conditions apply to different product types.

## Standard Return Window

Customers have **30 days from the date of delivery** to initiate a return for any reason. To be eligible for a full refund, items must be:

- In original, unused condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- Accompanied by the original order confirmation or invoice number

Items that show signs of use, washing, assembly, field deployment, or modification are subject to the restocking fee described below and may be refused at our discretion if damage is evident.

**Sale and clearance items** have a reduced return window of **15 days from the date of delivery** and are not eligible for exchange — store credit or refund only.

## Restocking Fee

A **15% restocking fee** applies to any item returned in opened but otherwise acceptable condition (packaging opened, tags removed, but item unused). This fee is waived for items returned due to a manufacturer defect, shipping damage, or a fulfillment error on our part.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:

- Climbing hardware (carabiners, cams, nuts, ice screws) once removed from packaging — safety equipment cannot be re-sold as new
- Custom or personalized gear (embroidered items, custom-fitted harnesses)
- Digital downloads and gift cards
- Fuel canisters (isobutane, white gas, alcohol) once opened
- Items returned more than 30 days after delivery (or 15 days for sale items)

## Warranty vs. Return

This policy covers change-of-mind and satisfaction returns. If your product fails due to a manufacturing defect within its warranty period, please use the **Warranty Claim process** described in the Warranty Policy document — that process may result in repair, replacement, or refund at Acme's discretion and is not subject to the 30-day window or restocking fee.

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to the Acme Outfitters customer portal at support.acmeoutfitters.example.
2. Locate your order and select **"Start a Return."**
3. Select the items you wish to return and provide a brief reason.
4. Print the prepaid return shipping label provided (domestic US customers only; international customers are responsible for return shipping costs).
5. Drop the package at any UPS location within **7 days** of receiving your return label. Labels expire after 7 days.

Returns not using the portal-generated label are accepted, but the customer is responsible for return shipping costs and tracking, and Acme is not liable for packages lost in transit.

## Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed as follows:

| Refund Method | Timeline |
|---|---|
| Original credit/debit card | 5–7 business days after inspection |
| ACH / bank transfer | 7–10 business days after inspection |
| PayPal | 3–5 business days after inspection |
| Acme store credit | 1–2 business days after inspection (instant if requested at time of return) |
| Check (original payment by check) | 10–14 business days after inspection |

We will send an email confirmation when the refund is initiated. Actual posting time depends on your financial institution.

## Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges (i.e., swapping one item for another of a different size or color). Instead, return the original item using this process and place a new order for the desired item. If you wish to hold the new item while the return is in transit, contact Support to request a temporary hold.

## Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive an item that was damaged during shipping, or if we sent you the wrong item, contact support within **48 hours of delivery** with photos. We will arrange a return at no cost and ship the correct or replacement item immediately, typically with expedited shipping at no additional charge.

## Questions

For questions about a specific return, contact our Support team through the customer portal or email support@acmeoutfitters.example.
