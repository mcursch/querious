# Return & Refund Policy — Acme Outfitters

_Last updated: January 15, 2025_

---

## 1. Overview

Acme Outfitters stands behind the quality of every product we sell. If you are not completely
satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return and refund process subject to the
terms described below.

---

## 2. Standard Return Window

Customers have **30 calendar days** from the date of delivery to initiate a return. The item must
be received at our warehouse within **45 calendar days** of delivery; returns that arrive after the
45-day hard deadline will be refused and shipped back to the customer at their expense.

The return window begins on the date shown in the delivery confirmation email, not the order date.

---

## 3. Condition Requirements

To qualify for a full refund the item must be:

- **Unworn and unwashed** — tags still attached, no visible signs of field use.
- **Unassembled or reassembled in original configuration** — tents, stoves, and frames must be
  returned in the original bag or box with all poles, stakes, and hardware included.
- **Free of odor, pet hair, and dirt.**

Items returned in a "lightly used / tried once" condition may be accepted at Acme Outfitters'
discretion and are subject to the restocking fee described in §4.

---

## 4. Restocking Fee

A **15 % restocking fee** is deducted from the refund amount in the following situations:

| Situation | Fee |
|---|---|
| Item returned in used or opened condition (non-defective) | 15 % of item price |
| Return initiated between day 31 and day 45 of the delivery window | 15 % of item price |
| Missing original packaging (box, bag, hang-tags) | 15 % of item price |

The restocking fee is **waived** when:

- The return is due to a manufacturing defect confirmed by our Quality team.
- Acme Outfitters shipped the wrong item.
- The item arrived damaged in transit (photo evidence required within 72 hours of delivery).

---

## 5. Non-Returnable Items (Exclusions)

The following product categories and situations are **not eligible for return or refund** under any
circumstances:

1. **Consumable fuel products** — isobutane/propane canisters, white-gas fuel bottles, and all
   other pressurized fuel containers.
2. **Customized or embroidered items** — any product with a name, logo, or monogram applied at the
   customer's request after the original order was placed.
3. **Avalanche safety devices** — avalanche transceivers (beacons), probes, and airbag packs, once
   removed from manufacturer-sealed packaging.
4. **Hygiene-sensitive items** — baselayer underwear and sports bras that have been tried on without
   the original protective liner.
5. **Digital goods** — route maps, guidebook downloads, and gear-care tutorial video licenses.
6. **Clearance items marked "Final Sale"** at time of purchase.

---

## 6. How to Initiate a Return

### Step 1 — Submit a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA)

Go to **acmeoutfitters.example/returns** or call **1-800-555-2267** (Mon–Fri 8 AM–6 PM MT) and
provide your order number and reason for return. An RMA number will be emailed within **1 business
day**.

### Step 2 — Pack and Ship

Write the RMA number clearly on the outside of the box. You may use any carrier; however, Acme
Outfitters recommends **UPS** or **FedEx** for tracking reliability. Original shipping charges are
not refunded unless the return is due to our error or a defect.

> **Tip:** For items valued over $150, purchase insurance from your carrier. Acme Outfitters is not
> responsible for packages lost in return transit without insurance.

### Step 3 — Processing

Returns are inspected within **3 business days** of warehouse receipt. You will receive an email
confirmation once the inspection is complete.

---

## 7. Refund Timeline

| Refund Method | Business Days After Inspection Approval |
|---|---|
| Original credit / debit card | 3 – 5 business days |
| PayPal | 1 – 2 business days |
| Acme Outfitters store credit | Same business day |
| Check (when card is no longer valid) | 7 – 10 business days |

Refunds are processed in the currency of the original transaction. For international orders paid in
a foreign currency, the refunded amount reflects the exchange rate at the time of the original
charge; Acme Outfitters does not absorb currency fluctuation losses.

---

## 8. Exchanges

We do not process direct exchanges. To get a different size, color, or model, initiate a return for
a refund and place a new order. If a replacement item is in stock, we recommend ordering it
immediately to avoid a stockout while your return is processed.

---

## 9. Defective or Damaged Goods

If an item arrives damaged or develops a manufacturing defect:

- **Damage in transit:** Report within **72 hours** of delivery by emailing
  **returns@acmeoutfitters.example** with photos. We will issue a prepaid return label and a full
  refund (no restocking fee) or replacement at your choice.
- **Manufacturing defect discovered after use:** See the **Warranty Policy** for coverage terms.
  Warranty claims are handled separately from this return policy.

---

## 10. Gift Returns

Items received as gifts may be returned for store credit only. The original purchaser's information
is required for verification; however, store credit will be issued to the gift recipient's account
upon their request.

---

## 11. Large-Order and Corporate Returns

Orders of 10 or more units of the same SKU placed under an enterprise or corporate account are
subject to a separate **Commercial Return Agreement**. Contact your dedicated account manager or
email **enterprise@acmeoutfitters.example** to initiate a large-order return. The standard 30-day
window still applies unless a custom window was negotiated in your Master Purchase Agreement.

---

## 12. Policy Amendments

Acme Outfitters reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes take effect
immediately upon posting. The date of last update appears at the top of this document.
