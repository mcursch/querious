# Customer Pricing Tiers — Acme Outfitters

_Last updated: January 15, 2025_

---

## 1. Overview

Acme Outfitters offers three customer account tiers — **Standard**, **Premium**, and
**Enterprise** — designed to reward loyalty and to meet the purchasing needs of individual outdoor
enthusiasts, frequent buyers, and commercial/institutional buyers alike. This document describes
the pricing benefits, discounts, and services associated with each tier.

## 3. Standard Tier

### Eligibility
All registered customers begin on Standard tier at no cost.

### Pricing
Standard-tier customers pay the full listed retail price on all products. No automatic discount
is applied.

### Free Shipping
Orders of **$75.00 or more** (after discount codes, before tax) ship free via Standard Ground to
the contiguous United States.

### Loyalty Points
Earn **1 point per $1.00** spent (excluding shipping and tax). Points may be redeemed at a rate
of **100 points = $1.00** store credit, with a minimum redemption of 500 points ($5.00).

### Support
Standard SLA (see SLA document): first response within 8 business hours for Urgent tickets; up to
5 business days for Low-priority tickets.

## 4. Premium Tier

### Eligibility
Any customer may enroll in Premium by paying the annual fee of **$49.00** at checkout or on the
Account page. The membership renews automatically each year unless canceled.

A customer who spends **$1,500 or more in a single calendar year** on the Standard tier is
automatically upgraded to Premium for the following year **at no charge**.

### Pricing Discount
Premium members receive a **10 % discount** on all Acme Outfitters-branded products. The discount
is applied automatically at checkout to the subtotal of eligible items. The following are
**excluded** from the Premium discount:

- Third-party brand products (brands not manufactured or private-labeled by Acme Outfitters)
- Gift cards and store credit purchases
- Items already marked as Clearance / Final Sale
- Bundles where a custom discounted price has already been applied

### Free Shipping
All orders ship free via Standard Ground regardless of order total. Standard Ground is upgraded to
**Expedited Ground at no charge** when the order total (after Premium discount, before tax) exceeds
**$200.00**.

### Loyalty Points
Earn **1.5 points per $1.00** spent. Redemption rate is unchanged (100 points = $1.00).

### Early Access
Premium members receive **48-hour early access** to new product launches. An email notification is
sent to the account email address 48 hours before the public launch date.

### Price-Match Guarantee
Premium members are eligible for a price match if an identical Acme Outfitters product is
available at a lower price from an authorized competitor. To request a price match:

1. Contact **support@acmeoutfitters.example** with the competitor URL.
2. Price match is honored if the lower price is verified and the competitor is in stock.
3. Maximum discount applied via price match may not exceed **20 % below** the Acme Outfitters
   retail price (i.e., Premium + price match cannot compound beyond a 20 % total discount).

### Returns
Standard 30-day return window applies. Premium members receive **free return shipping** (prepaid
label emailed on request) for one return per calendar quarter; additional returns in the same
quarter use standard return procedures (customer pays return shipping).

### Support
Premium SLA: first response within 4 business hours for Urgent tickets; up to 2 business days for
Low-priority tickets. Live Chat support available. (See SLA document for full matrix.)

## 5. Enterprise Tier

### Eligibility
Enterprise tier is available to businesses, government agencies, educational institutions,
non-profits, and outfitters purchasing for commercial resale or fleet outfitting. Minimum annual
spend commitment is **$10,000**. Contact **enterprise@acmeoutfitters.example** to apply.

### Contract and Pricing
Enterprise pricing is governed by a **Master Purchase Agreement (MPA)** negotiated individually.
The standard starting discount for Enterprise customers is **20 % off all products** (including
third-party brands carried by Acme Outfitters, unlike Premium which excludes third-party brands).

Additional volume discounts are available based on annual spend:

| Annual Spend (contracted) | Additional Discount Beyond Base 20 % |
|---|---|
| $10,000 – $24,999 | 0 % (base 20 % applies) |
| $25,000 – $49,999 | +3 % (total effective discount: 23 %) |
| $50,000 – $99,999 | +5 % (total effective discount: 25 %) |
| $100,000 + | +8 % (total effective discount: 28 %) |

**Custom SKU pricing** is available for Enterprise customers who commit to purchasing a minimum
quantity (typically 25 units per SKU per year). Custom pricing is documented in a line-item
schedule attached to the MPA.

### Payment Terms
Enterprise customers invoiced under an MPA have **Net-30** payment terms. Orders are shipped upon
approval of a purchase order (PO); no credit card required. Late payments (past 30 days) incur a
**1.5 % monthly finance charge**.

### Free Shipping
All orders ship free via **Expedited Ground** (3 – 5 business days). For orders over **$5,000**
(per shipment), free 2-Day Air is available on request; contact your account manager.

### Loyalty Points
Earn **2 points per $1.00** spent. Enterprise customers may also direct earned points to a
company-wide pool redeemable by any employee on the account.

### Early Access and Pre-Release
Enterprise customers receive a **2-week exclusive pre-release window** for new products, plus the
option to place pre-production minimum orders at locked-in pricing.

### Returns
Extended **45-day return window** (vs. 30 days for Standard and Premium). Commercial return
procedures apply for orders of 10+ units of the same SKU (see Return Policy §11).

### Dedicated Account Manager
Each Enterprise account is assigned a dedicated Acme Outfitters Account Manager who:
- Provides quarterly business reviews
- Manages custom orders and bulk pricing requests
- Coordinates with our Warehouse team for scheduled bulk shipments
- Is reachable by direct email and phone (no queue)

### Support
Enterprise SLA: first response within **1 calendar hour** (24/7) for Urgent tickets; 2 business
hours for High-priority tickets. Dedicated phone queue, live chat, and email support. Monthly SLA
report provided. (See SLA document for full matrix.)

## 6. Comparing Total Cost of Ownership

To illustrate the value of each tier, consider a customer spending **$3,000 per year**:

| Tier | Annual Fee | Product Discount (10 % or 20 %) | Shipping Savings (est.) | Net Annual Value vs. Standard |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Standard | $0 | $0 | ~$0 (threshold met often) | baseline |
| Premium | $49 | $300 (10 % of $3,000) | ~$35 (free ground, no minimums) | +$286 net |
| Enterprise | Contract | $600 (20 % of $3,000) | ~$80 (expedited ground free) | +$680 net (before contract terms) |

_These are illustrative estimates. Actual savings vary based on product mix, shipping frequency,
and negotiated MPA terms._

## 7. Tier Changes

- **Upgrading:** Takes effect immediately upon payment or contract execution. The discount is
  applied to all orders placed after the upgrade, not retroactively.
- **Downgrading or canceling Premium:** Downgrade takes effect at the end of the current annual
  membership period. Orders already placed retain the discount; future orders revert to Standard
  pricing.
- **Losing auto-upgrade Premium status:** If an auto-upgraded Premium customer does not spend
  $1,500 in the following calendar year and does not pay the $49 annual fee, they revert to
  Standard at the end of that year.

---

## 8. Stacking Rules

Discounts **do not stack** beyond the negotiated tier discount except as follows:

- **Promotional discount codes:** Promotional codes may be applied on top of a tier discount
  **only** when the promotion explicitly states "stackable." By default, promotional codes are
  non-stackable with tier discounts (the higher of the two discounts applies).
- **Loyalty point redemption:** Always stackable with tier discounts.
- **Price-match adjustments (Premium and Enterprise):** Applied after tier discount.
