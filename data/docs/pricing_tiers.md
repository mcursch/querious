# Customer Pricing Tiers

*Effective January 1, 2025.*

## Overview

Acme Outfitters offers three customer tiers: **Standard**, **Premium**, and **Enterprise**. Tier assignment is based on annual spend and account type. Each tier unlocks a progressively richer set of discounts, shipping benefits, and service perks. Tier status is reviewed quarterly; upgrades take effect immediately while downgrades take effect at the start of the next calendar quarter.

---

## Tier Definitions

### Standard Tier

**Who qualifies:** All new customers and returning customers whose rolling 12-month spend is below $500.

Standard is the default tier for new accounts and represents the full published retail prices with no automatic discount.

**Benefits:**
- Full access to the Acme Outfitters catalog
- Standard return window (30 days — see `return_refund_policy.md`)
- Free standard ground shipping on orders over $75
- Access to Support team via email and phone (standard SLA — see `sla.md`)
- Early access to seasonal sales events (48-hour head start on public promotions)

### Premium Tier

**Who qualifies:** Customers whose rolling 12-month spend is **$500–$4,999** or individual retail customers who choose to enroll in the Acme Outfitters Membership Program for $49/year.

**Benefits:**
- **10% discount** on all full-price merchandise (stacked before tax; not applicable to already-discounted clearance items or gift cards)
- Free standard ground shipping on **all orders** (no minimum)
- Priority processing — orders placed before 4:00 PM local DC time ship same day (vs. 2:00 PM for Standard)
- Extended return window: **45 days** (vs. 30 days)
- Access to the Gear Lending Library (demo tents and packs available to borrow for weekend trips)
- Dedicated phone queue with shorter hold times (see `sla.md`)
- Advance access to new product launches (7 days before general availability)

### Enterprise Tier

**Who qualifies:** Organizations or business accounts with annual contracted spend of **$5,000 or more**, or any account approved by the VP of Sales for strategic partner status.

Enterprise accounts are managed by a dedicated account executive and have a negotiated master agreement in addition to these standard benefits.

**Benefits:**
- **15–25% discount** on all merchandise (exact rate negotiated in account agreement; typically 15% for $5K–$19K spend, 20% for $20K–$49K, and 25% for $50K+)
- Free shipping on all orders, including expedited methods (standard ground and 2-day are fully covered; overnight shipping above $0 per-shipment base rate applies only to accounts with overnight included in their agreement)
- Dedicated account executive with direct phone and email access
- Net-30 invoicing terms (all other tiers pay at checkout)
- Custom purchase-order (PO) workflow for procurement departments
- Extended return window: **60 days** on all items
- Priority SLA with 1-hour response for urgent tickets (see `sla.md`)
- Quarterly business reviews with the VP of Sales or Customer Experience
- Access to volume-based custom kitting (co-branded gear for corporate programs, guide services, camps, etc.)
- Warranty extensions as negotiated (see `warranty_policy.md`)

## Discount Stacking Rules

Discounts across tiers interact as follows:

1. **Tier discount** is applied first to the full retail price.
2. **Promotional discounts** (site-wide sales, coupon codes) are applied to the post-tier price if the promotion terms say "stackable." Non-stackable promotions use the higher of the two discounts, not both.
3. **Volume discounts** (10+ units of the same SKU) are applied after the tier discount. Volume discount rates are published on the product page for eligible SKUs.
4. **Clearance prices** already reflect a markdown from MSRP. Tier discounts do not apply to clearance-tagged items unless stated otherwise in a promotion.
5. Gift cards are sold at face value; no tier discount applies to gift-card purchases.

## Tier Upgrades and Downgrades

### Upgrades

Customers are automatically upgraded to the next tier as soon as their rolling 12-month spend crosses the threshold. The new tier pricing and benefits apply to the next order placed after upgrade.

Customers who manually enroll in the Premium Membership Program are upgraded immediately upon payment of the $49 annual fee, regardless of spend history.

### Downgrades

Automatic downgrades are evaluated at the end of each calendar quarter (March 31, June 30, September 30, December 31). If a customer's rolling 12-month spend has fallen below the current tier threshold, they are moved to the lower tier effective the first day of the following quarter. Customers will receive an email notification 30 days before a downgrade takes effect.

Enterprise accounts with a signed annual contract retain Enterprise tier status for the contract duration regardless of spend fluctuations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can a Standard customer get the Premium discount on a single large order?

No. The tier discount applies only to customers who hold the qualifying tier at the time of purchase. However, if that purchase pushes a customer's rolling 12-month spend above the threshold, they will be automatically upgraded to Premium and receive the benefit on subsequent orders.

### Does the Acme Membership Program fee count toward spend for tier purposes?

No. The $49 annual membership fee does not count toward the $500 spend threshold for automatic Premium qualification; it purchases Premium status directly.

### How are tiers handled for B2B accounts with multiple buyers?

Enterprise accounts with a PO workflow can add unlimited buyer accounts to a single master account, and all purchases roll up to the master account for spend tracking and tier evaluation.
