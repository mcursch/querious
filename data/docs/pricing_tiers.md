# Customer Pricing Tiers — Acme Outfitters

## Overview

Acme Outfitters uses a three-tier customer model — **Standard**, **Premium**, and **Enterprise** — to provide benefits scaled to purchase volume and relationship depth. Tier assignment is made by the Sales team at account creation and reviewed annually. Customers can request a tier review at any time by contacting their account manager or emailing sales@acmeoutfitters.example.

Tier designations are stored on each customer account and drive automatic pricing and support routing throughout our systems.

## Tier Definitions & Benefits

### Standard Tier

The Standard tier is the default for all new customers and for individual consumers with no formal account agreement.

| Benefit | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product discount | **No standing discount** — catalog price applies |
| Free shipping threshold | $75.00 order minimum |
| Payment terms | Payment due at time of order (card/PayPal) |
| Invoice payment | Available upon request with approved credit application |
| Support SLA | Standard response times (see SLA document) |
| Dedicated account manager | Not included |
| Early product access | Not included |

Standard tier customers have full access to the Acme online store and can use promotional codes and seasonal sales as published.

### Premium Tier

The Premium tier is available to customers who maintain an average annual spend of **$2,500 or more** or who operate a small outdoor guide service, retail shop, or similar business with a verified reseller or business account.

| Benefit | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product discount | **10% off catalog price** on all non-sale items |
| Free shipping threshold | **$50.00** order minimum (reduced from Standard) |
| Payment terms | Net-15 invoicing available (invoices due 15 days after issue) |
| Support SLA | Accelerated response times (see SLA document) |
| Dedicated account manager | Shared account manager (handles up to 20 Premium accounts) |
| Early product access | Invited to seasonal preview sales 2 weeks before public launch |
| Annual catalog | Printed catalog mailed each January |

Premium customers receive a **10% discount** applied automatically at checkout — no code required. This discount does not stack with promotional codes; the higher of the two discounts applies.

### Enterprise Tier

The Enterprise tier is a negotiated account relationship intended for large outdoor retailers, guide outfitters, corporate wellness programs, expedition outfitters, and institutional buyers with significant annual volume.

| Benefit | Detail |
|---|---|
| Product discount | **20% off catalog price** on standard catalog items; custom pricing available for volume orders |
| Free shipping threshold | **Free standard shipping on all orders** (no minimum) |
| Expedited shipping | One free 2-day Air upgrade per quarter |
| Payment terms | Net-30 invoicing standard; Net-45 available with CFO approval |
| Support SLA | Priority response times — fastest available (see SLA document) |
| Dedicated account manager | Dedicated named account manager |
| Early product access | Invited to new-product briefings 4 weeks before public launch; sample program available |
| Custom labeling | Co-branded product packaging available for orders of 50+ units per SKU |
| Quarterly business reviews | Formal QBR meeting with Sales and Finance |

Enterprise pricing is governed by a signed Enterprise Agreement. The **20% base discount** applies to all standard-catalog items. Volume pricing (25%+ discount) is available for single-SKU purchase orders of 100 units or more and requires a quote from the Sales team.

## Tier Upgrade & Downgrade

**Upgrading:** A customer meeting the spend or business criteria for a higher tier may request an upgrade at any time. The Sales team will verify eligibility and apply the new tier within 2 business days. The discount applies to orders placed after the tier change — it is not applied retroactively to recent orders.

**Downgrading:** Tiers are reviewed each January 1. If a customer's trailing-12-month spend falls below the Premium threshold, they are notified in December and downgraded to Standard on January 1 unless they commit to a volume agreement. Enterprise customers are governed by their signed agreement terms.

## Pricing on Sale Items

Tier discounts apply to **catalog (non-sale) pricing only**. If an item is already marked down in a sale event, the tier discount does not apply additionally. Customers will always receive the better of their tier discount or the sale price — they never pay more than either would suggest.

## Tax & Compliance

All prices listed are exclusive of applicable sales tax. Acme Outfitters is required to collect and remit sales tax in states where we have nexus. Business customers claiming sales-tax exemption must provide a valid exemption certificate to the Finance team before placing their first order.
