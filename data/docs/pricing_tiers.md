# Customer Pricing Tiers

## Overview

Acme Outfitters offers three customer tiers — **Standard**, **Premium**, and **Enterprise** —
each with increasing benefits and discounts. Tier assignment is managed by the Sales team.

## Tier Comparison

| Benefit | Standard | Premium | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Base discount on MSRP | 0% | 10% | 20% |
| Free shipping threshold | $75 | Always free (ground) | Always free (2-day) |
| Return window | 30 days | 30 days | 60 days |
| Dedicated account manager | No | No | Yes |
| Net payment terms | Net 0 (pay at purchase) | Net 15 | Net 30 |
| Volume discount (orders >$1,000) | None | Additional 5% | Additional 10% |
| Early access to new products | No | 7-day early access | 14-day early access |
| Priority support SLA | Standard | Enhanced | Premium |
| Annual spend minimum | None | $500/year | $5,000/year |

## Standard Tier

Standard is the default tier for all new customers. No minimum spend is required.

**Benefits:**
- Full access to all products at MSRP
- Free economy shipping on orders $75+
- Standard 30-day return window
- Standard customer support response times

## Premium Tier

Premium customers are individual buyers or small businesses with demonstrated spend of $500
or more per year. Tier upgrade requests can be initiated by the customer or by the assigned
Sales Rep.

**Additional benefits over Standard:**
- 10% discount on all orders
- Free standard ground shipping on all orders
- Additional 5% volume discount on single orders over $1,000 (stacks with base discount)
- Net-15 payment terms available on request
- 7-day early access to new product launches
- Enhanced support SLA (see `sla.md`)

**Upgrade process:** Contact sales@acmeoutfitters.example or ask your Sales Rep.

## Enterprise Tier

Enterprise is designed for businesses, outfitters, guide services, schools, and government
agencies with significant annual purchasing needs. Minimum annual spend of $5,000 is expected.

**Additional benefits over Premium:**
- 20% discount on all orders
- Free 2-day expedited shipping on all orders
- Additional 10% volume discount on single orders over $1,000 (stacks with base 20%)
- Net-30 payment terms (subject to credit approval)
- 14-day early access to new product launches
- Extended 60-day return window
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom SKU catalogs and bulk ordering support
- Quarterly business reviews with account team
- Premium support SLA (see `sla.md`)

**Upgrade process:** Contact enterprise@acmeoutfitters.example. A Sales team member will
conduct a brief needs assessment.

## Discount Stacking Rules

Discounts stack in this order:
1. Tier base discount (10% Premium / 20% Enterprise)
2. Volume discount on eligible orders (5% Premium / 10% Enterprise)
3. Promotional codes (do not stack with each other; apply after tier discounts)

Example: An Enterprise customer places an order for $1,500.
- Base discount: 20% → $1,200
- Volume discount: 10% of $1,200 → $120 off → $1,080
- Promo code (if any): applied to $1,080

## Tier Review & Downgrade

Tiers are reviewed annually in Q4. If a Premium or Enterprise customer does not meet the
minimum spend threshold for the year, their Account Manager will reach out to discuss
continued tier eligibility. Downgrades take effect January 1 of the following year.
