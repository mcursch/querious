# Customer Pricing Tiers

## Tier Overview

| Tier | Requirements | Discount |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Default for all new accounts | None |
| Premium | $5,000+ annual spend | 10% on all orders |
| Enterprise | $25,000+ annual spend or negotiated contract | 15–20% (negotiated) |

## Benefits by Tier

### Standard
- Access to all products
- Standard shipping rates
- Email support

### Premium
- 10% discount on all orders
- Free standard shipping on all orders (no minimum)
- Priority support queue
- Early access to new products

### Enterprise
- Negotiated discount (15–20%)
- Free expedited shipping
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom billing terms
- 24/7 phone support
