# Support Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Overview

This document defines Acme Outfitters' support response-time commitments to customers across
all ticket priorities and customer tiers.

## Support Channels

| Channel | Hours | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Email (support@acmeoutfitters.example) | 24/7 intake; staffed Mon–Fri 8 AM–6 PM MT | Responses during staffed hours |
| Phone (1-800-226-3688) | Mon–Fri 8 AM–6 PM MT | Live agents during business hours |
| Live Chat (website) | Mon–Fri 9 AM–5 PM MT | Premium and Enterprise only |
| Dedicated Account Manager | Business hours | Enterprise only |

## Ticket Priority Definitions

| Priority | Definition | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Urgent** | Business-critical issue preventing core operations | Payment system failure, bulk order corruption, safety recall |
| **High** | Significant issue with no immediate workaround | Order stuck in processing >48h, shipping system error, major billing discrepancy |
| **Medium** | Issue with a workaround available | Return label not generating, incorrect item in shipment, account access issue |
| **Low** | Minor inconvenience or general inquiry | Product question, policy clarification, feedback |

## Response Time Commitments

### First Response Time (time to acknowledgment from a support agent)

| Priority | Standard | Premium | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Urgent | 8 business hours | 4 business hours | 1 business hour |
| High | 2 business days | 1 business day | 4 business hours |
| Medium | 3 business days | 2 business days | 1 business day |
| Low | 5 business days | 3 business days | 2 business days |

### Resolution Time (time from first response to issue closed)

| Priority | Standard | Premium | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| Urgent | 3 business days | 2 business days | Same business day |
| High | 5 business days | 3 business days | 2 business days |
| Medium | 10 business days | 7 business days | 3 business days |
| Low | 15 business days | 10 business days | 5 business days |

## SLA Measurement

- "Business hours" = 8 AM–6 PM Mountain Time, Monday–Friday, excluding US federal holidays.
- SLA clock starts when the ticket is received by our system (not when it is read by an agent).
- The clock pauses when we are waiting on a customer response (status: "Awaiting Customer").
- Tickets submitted outside business hours have the clock start at 8 AM the next business day.

## Escalation Policy

If an SLA is at risk (75% of the time window has elapsed without resolution), the ticket is
automatically flagged and escalated to the Support Lead for that priority tier.

Enterprise customers may request direct escalation to their Account Manager at any time by
responding to the ticket with "ESCALATE" in the subject line.

## SLA Credits

If Acme Outfitters fails to meet an SLA for an Urgent or High priority ticket, the affected
customer is eligible for a service credit:

| Breach | Credit |
|---|---|
| First response SLA missed by 50%+ | $25 store credit |
| Resolution SLA missed by 50%+ | $50 store credit |
| Both missed on same ticket | $75 store credit |

Credits are applied automatically to the customer account once the ticket is closed.
Standard-tier customers are not currently eligible for SLA credits.

## Out-of-Scope Support

The following are handled separately and do not follow the above SLA timelines:

- Warranty claims (see `warranty_policy.md`) — handled by Warranty Team, 3-business-day
  first response
- Billing disputes involving more than $500 — escalated to Finance; 5-business-day first response
- Safety-related issues (product recalls, climbing gear failure) — immediate escalation, 24/7
