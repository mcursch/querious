# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Support Response-Time Commitments

Acme Outfitters commits to the following response times for customer support tickets based on ticket priority and customer tier.

## Priority Definitions

| Priority | Description |
|---|---|
| **Urgent** | System outage, complete inability to access account, or financial discrepancy |
| **High** | Major feature unavailable, order processing blocked, or safety concern |
| **Medium** | Significant inconvenience, partial functionality impacted |
| **Low** | General questions, cosmetic issues, non-blocking requests |

## Response Time SLA by Customer Tier

### Standard Customers

| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| Urgent | 8 business hours | 3 business days |
| High | 24 business hours | 5 business days |
| Medium | 48 business hours | 10 business days |
| Low | 72 business hours | 15 business days |

### Premium Customers

Premium customers receive **prioritized queue placement** and dedicated support agents.

| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| Urgent | **2 business hours** | 1 business day |
| High | **4 business hours** | 2 business days |
| Medium | **8 business hours** | 5 business days |
| Low | **24 business hours** | 7 business days |

### Enterprise Customers

Enterprise customers receive a dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM) and 24/7 phone support for urgent issues.

| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|
| Urgent | **30 minutes** (24/7) | Same business day |
| High | **1 business hour** | 1 business day |
| Medium | **4 business hours** | 3 business days |
| Low | **8 business hours** | 5 business days |

## SLA Measurement

- "First Response" = time from ticket creation to first substantive agent reply (auto-acknowledgements do not count).
- "Resolution Target" = time from ticket creation to status change to `resolved` or `closed`.
- Business hours = 8 AM–6 PM Mountain Time, Monday–Friday, excluding federal holidays.
- Enterprise 24/7 urgent support covers all hours including weekends and holidays.

## SLA Credits

If Acme Outfitters fails to meet the first-response SLA, eligible customers may request a credit:

| Tier | Credit (per SLA miss) |
|---|---|
| Premium | 5% off next invoice |
| Enterprise | 10% off next invoice, escalation to VP of Support |

Standard customers are not eligible for SLA credits but may escalate to a manager.

## Escalation Path

1. **Level 1**: Support Agent (all initial contacts)
2. **Level 2**: Senior Support Specialist (after first-response SLA miss, or customer request)
3. **Level 3**: Support Manager (after resolution SLA miss, or repeated escalations)
4. **Level 4**: VP of Support (Enterprise only, or unresolved after Level 3)
