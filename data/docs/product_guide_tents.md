# Product Guide: Tents

## Tent Line Overview

Acme Outfitters offers three tent lines: TrailLite, AlpineShield, and BaseCamp.

### TrailLite Series
Ultralight backpacking tents. Single-wall construction with silnylon fabric.
- Weight: 1.8–2.5 lbs
- Seasons: 3-season
- Care: Wipe clean; never machine wash; store dry

### AlpineShield Series
Four-season mountaineering tents with geodesic pole structure.
- Weight: 4.5–6.2 lbs
- Seasons: 4-season
- Care: Reproofing spray annually; check seam tape yearly

### BaseCamp Series
Car-camping tents with easy setup and large interior volume.
- Weight: 8–14 lbs
- Seasons: 3-season
- Care: Machine wash on gentle with tent-specific cleaner
