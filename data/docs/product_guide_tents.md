product line. SKUs follow the pattern `ACM-TENT-###`. Information here supplements the product
database (price, stock) with technical details that support informed purchasing decisions.

## Care & Maintenance

### Cleaning
- Shake out loose debris before packing. Never pack a wet tent — mold and delamination will result.
- Spot-clean body and fly with a soft sponge, cold water, and non-detergent soap. Avoid bleach,
  alcohol, or petroleum-based solvents.
- For heavier soiling, hand-wash in a bathtub with tent-specific cleaner (e.g., Nikwax Tech Wash).
  Do **not** machine-wash tents with aluminum poles — the agitation bends poles.
- Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap residue, which degrades DWR and PU coatings.

### Drying
- Pitch or hang the tent in a shaded, well-ventilated area. UV exposure degrades nylon and polyester.
- Never dry in a clothes dryer.

### Seam Sealing (ACM-TENT-001 and ACM-TENT-003 only)
- Factory seam tape is not applied to these models. Reseal interior seams before first use and
  every 2–3 seasons thereafter, or when you notice weeping at stitch lines.
- Use Seam Grip WP or equivalent urethane sealer on the interior seams of the fly and floor.
- Allow 24 hours cure time before packing.

### DWR Refresh
- When water stops beading on the fly, reapply DWR (e.g., Nikwax Tent & Gear SolarProof).
- Apply after washing while fabric is still slightly damp for best uptake.

### Pole Care
- Inspect poles after each trip. Repair minor bends with a pole-repair sleeve.
- Keep elastic shock-cord tension firm; replace shock cord when it goes slack. Replacement shock
  cord is available from Acme Outfitters customer support.
- Wipe aluminum poles with a dry cloth before storage to prevent oxidation.

### Storage
- Store loosely in a breathable cotton or mesh bag — never compressed in the stuff sack long-term,
  as this fatigues the coatings and compresses the seams.
- Keep in a cool, dry, dark location away from rodents and prolonged sunlight.
- Store tent pegs separately to avoid puncturing the fabric.

### Zipper Maintenance
- Apply wax-based zipper lubricant to zipper teeth once per season.
- Never force a stuck zipper; remove debris with a soft brush before trying again.

## Warranty

All Acme Outfitters tents carry a **2-year manufacturing defect warranty** from the date of
purchase. See `warranty_policy.md` for full claim procedures. Damage from UV degradation, improper
care, or improper storage is not covered under warranty.
