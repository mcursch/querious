# Shipping Policy

*Effective January 1, 2025.*

## Overview

Acme Outfitters ships from three distribution centers: Portland OR, Denver CO, and Nashville TN. Orders are automatically routed to the DC with available inventory that minimizes transit time to the destination. This document describes shipping methods, costs, estimated transit times, and special rules for international and oversized shipments.

---

## Domestic Shipping

### Carriers

Acme Outfitters ships via **UPS** and **USPS** for standard and expedited domestic orders. Heavy or oversized items (tent poles over 42", frame packs, canoes) may ship via **FedEx Freight**. The carrier is selected automatically at checkout based on package dimensions, weight, and delivery address.

### Shipping Methods and Costs

| Method | Estimated Transit | Base Cost |
|--------|------------------|-----------|
| Standard Ground | 5–7 business days | $7.95 |
| Expedited (2-Day) | 2 business days | $19.95 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $39.95 |
| Freight (oversized) | 4–10 business days | Quoted at checkout |

### Free Shipping Threshold

Orders with a merchandise subtotal of **$75 or more** (before tax, after discounts) qualify for free standard ground shipping to any address in the contiguous 48 states. Alaska, Hawaii, and US territories do not qualify for the free-shipping threshold.

Premium and Enterprise tier customers have their free-shipping threshold reduced to **$0** — all orders ship free via standard ground (see `pricing_tiers.md` for tier details).

### Order Cut-Off Times

Orders placed by **2:00 PM local time** at the fulfilling DC on a business day ship the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM, or on weekends and holidays, ship the next business day. "Business day" excludes the 11 Acme Outfitters holidays listed in the Employee Handbook.

---

## Shipping Zones and Transit Times

The continental US is divided into five shipping zones based on distance from the nearest Acme DC. Zone 1 is the area surrounding each DC; Zone 5 is the farthest coast.

| Zone | Typical Destination Examples | Standard Ground Transit |
|------|------------------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | OR, WA, CO, ID, TN, AL | 1–2 business days |
| 2 | CA, NV, WY, KY, GA | 2–3 business days |
| 3 | AZ, UT, TX, FL, NC | 3–4 business days |
| 4 | MN, WI, IL, NY, MA | 4–5 business days |
| 5 | HI, AK, Puerto Rico | 7–10 business days (no free shipping) |

These are estimates; actual transit times depend on carrier network conditions and weather events.

---

## Alaska, Hawaii, and US Territories

Shipments to Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, and other US territories are accepted for standard items. Expedited and Overnight services are not available for these destinations. Shipping costs for these destinations are calculated at checkout and are not eligible for the free-shipping promotion.

Fuel canisters and compressed-gas items **cannot be shipped by air** and are therefore unavailable for shipment to Hawaii, Alaska, or US territories. Customers in those locations should source fuel locally.

## International Shipping

### Available Countries

Acme Outfitters ships internationally to Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, France, and Japan. Additional countries may be available for select items; the checkout page will display available shipping options.

### International Rates and Transit

| Region | Estimated Transit | Base Cost |
|--------|------------------|-----------|
| Canada | 5–10 business days | $24.95 |
| UK / EU | 10–18 business days | $49.95 |
| Australia / NZ | 12–21 business days | $59.95 |
| Japan | 10–15 business days | $49.95 |

### Customs, Duties, and Taxes

International orders are shipped **DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid)**. The recipient is responsible for all import duties, taxes, and customs brokerage fees assessed by the destination country. Acme Outfitters does not pre-collect these fees and cannot predict their amount. Refused shipments due to duty non-payment are credited as store credit minus return shipping costs.

### Restricted Items for International Shipment

Certain items cannot be shipped internationally due to carrier restrictions or customs regulations:
- Fuel canisters and compressed-gas products (all countries)
- Bear canisters that exceed carry-on airline dimensions (Australia, NZ)
- Knives with blades over 3" (UK, Japan)
- Pepper spray / bear spray (all countries)

---

## PO Boxes and Military Addresses (APO/FPO/DPO)

Standard and expedited shipping is available to APO/FPO/DPO addresses via USPS Priority Mail. Overnight and Freight services are not available for military addresses. Transit times to APO/FPO/DPO addresses are 7–21 business days depending on the unit's location.

---

## Order Tracking

A shipping confirmation email with a tracking link is sent when your order is picked up by the carrier. Tracking is available on the carrier's website and through your Acme Outfitters account at acmeoutfitters.example/account. Allow up to 24 hours after shipment for tracking information to appear in the carrier's system.

---

## Lost or Delayed Shipments

If your tracking shows no movement for more than **5 consecutive business days** after the estimated delivery date, contact support@acmeoutfitters.example. We will file a carrier claim on your behalf and ship a replacement (or issue a full refund if the item is out of stock) within 3 business days of confirming the claim.

---

## Address Changes

We cannot guarantee address changes after an order has been placed. Contact support immediately (within 1 hour of order placement) for the best chance of intercepting the order before it ships. We are not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided during checkout.
