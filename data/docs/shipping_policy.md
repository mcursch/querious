# Shipping Policy — Acme Outfitters

_Last updated: January 15, 2025_

---

## 1. Overview

Acme Outfitters ships to all 50 U.S. states, U.S. territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, U.S. Virgin
Islands, and American Samoa), and select international destinations. This document describes our
carriers, delivery zones, rates, and special handling rules.

---

## 2. Carriers

We partner with the following carriers. The carrier assigned to your shipment depends on your
delivery address and the service level selected at checkout.

| Carrier | Services Used |
|---|---|
| **UPS** | Ground, 2-Day Air, Next Day Air |
| **FedEx** | Ground, Express Saver (3-day), 2-Day, Overnight |
| **USPS** | Priority Mail, Priority Mail Express (rural / PO Box addresses) |
| **DHL Express** | International shipments |

Acme Outfitters reserves the right to substitute a comparable service from a different carrier at
no additional cost to the customer. The carrier confirmation and tracking number are emailed once
the label is created.

---

## 3. Processing Time

Orders are processed on business days (Monday through Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays).

- Orders placed before **2:00 PM MT** on a business day are typically processed the **same day**.
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM MT, or on a weekend or holiday, are processed the **next business
  day**.
- During peak periods (Black Friday week, December 10–23), processing may take up to **2 additional
  business days**.

Processing time is separate from transit time. A "2-Day Air" shipment processed on Tuesday departs
Tuesday and arrives Thursday.

**Orders totaling $75.00 or more** (after any discounts, before tax) qualify for free Standard
Ground shipping to the contiguous United States. This threshold applies per shipment, not per item.

Premium-tier customers receive free Standard Ground shipping on all orders regardless of order
total. Enterprise-tier customers receive free Expedited Ground shipping on all orders (see Pricing
Tiers document for full tier benefits).

### 4.3 Alaska and Hawaii

## 5. International Shipping

Acme Outfitters ships internationally via **DHL Express** to the following countries/regions:

- Canada
- United Kingdom
- European Union member states
- Australia and New Zealand
- Japan and South Korea

Other countries are not currently supported. International customers may use a U.S.-based freight
forwarding service at their own arrangement.

### 5.1 International Rates and Transit Times

| Destination | Service | Estimated Transit | Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Canada | DHL Express Worldwide | 3 – 5 business days | $29.95 |
| UK / EU | DHL Express Worldwide | 5 – 8 business days | $44.95 |
| Australia / NZ | DHL Express Worldwide | 7 – 10 business days | $54.95 |
| Japan / S. Korea | DHL Express Worldwide | 6 – 9 business days | $49.95 |

All international rates are for shipments up to **5 lbs**. Shipments over 5 lbs are quoted at
checkout based on actual DHL dimensional weight pricing.

### 5.2 Duties, Taxes, and Customs

International customers are responsible for all import duties, customs fees, and local taxes
assessed by the destination country. Acme Outfitters ships on **Delivered at Place (DAP)** terms.
DHL may collect these charges at delivery. Refusal to pay duties does not entitle the customer to a
refund of the original order; return shipping and any assessed fees will be deducted from any
eligible credit.

### 5.3 Restricted Items for International Shipment

The following items cannot be shipped internationally due to IATA hazardous materials rules or
destination country import restrictions:

- All pressurized fuel canisters and white-gas fuel
- Liquid fuel stoves that have been used (residual fuel contamination)
- Aerosol-based water repellent treatments
- Bear spray and wildlife deterrent products

## 7. Large or Oversize Items

Items with a combined length + girth exceeding **165 inches**, or a single-side length exceeding
**108 inches**, are classified as oversize. Examples include large base-camp tents, expedition
sleds, and canoe paddles.

Oversize shipments incur a **$25.00 oversize surcharge** in addition to standard shipping rates.
Next Day Air and 2-Day Air are not available for oversize items; maximum service is Expedited
Ground.

---

## 8. Hazardous Materials

Isobutane/propane fuel canisters and bear spray ship via **UPS Ground** only within the contiguous
United States; they may not ship to Alaska, Hawaii, territories, APO/FPO/DPO, P.O. Boxes, or
internationally. These items carry a **$3.00 hazmat handling fee** per shipment (not per item),
charged at checkout.

## 9. Order Tracking

A tracking number is emailed when the carrier label is created (usually 1 – 2 hours after
processing). Track your shipment at:

- UPS: **ups.com/track**
- FedEx: **fedex.com/tracking**
- USPS: **tools.usps.com/go/TrackConfirmAction**
- DHL: **dhl.com/tracking**

Allow up to **24 hours** after receiving the tracking email for the carrier's system to reflect the
first scan.

---

## 10. Lost or Stolen Packages

If tracking shows "Delivered" but you have not received your package:

1. Check with neighbors and building management.
2. Wait 24 hours — carriers occasionally update tracking prematurely.
3. Contact **support@acmeoutfitters.example** with your order number. We will open a carrier trace.

For packages confirmed lost in transit (no delivery scan within expected window), Acme Outfitters
will reship at no cost or issue a full refund. Carrier investigations can take up to **10 business
days**.

Acme Outfitters is not responsible for packages confirmed delivered to the correct address that are
subsequently stolen. We recommend filing a police report and checking your homeowner's or renter's
insurance for theft coverage.

## 11. Address Errors and Intercepts

If you realize your shipping address is incorrect after placing an order, contact us **immediately**
at **1-800-555-2267**. We can intercept or redirect a shipment before carrier pickup at no charge.
After carrier pickup, address corrections are subject to a **$15.00 intercept fee** charged by the
carrier and passed through to the customer.
