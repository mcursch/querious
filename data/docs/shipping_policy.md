# Shipping Policy

## Overview

Acme Outfitters ships to all 50 US states, US territories, and select international destinations.
All orders are processed from our Denver, CO warehouse.

## Domestic Shipping

### Carriers

We ship via UPS, FedEx, and USPS depending on package size, weight, and destination.

### Shipping Tiers

| Service | Estimated Delivery | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Ground | 5–8 business days | Default for most orders |
| Expedited | 2–3 business days | Available at checkout |
| Overnight | Next business day | Order by 1 PM MT for same-day processing |
| Free Economy | 7–10 business days | Qualifies when eligible (see below) |

### Free Shipping Threshold

- **Standard customers:** Free economy shipping on orders of $75 or more (before tax).
- **Premium customers:** Free standard ground shipping on all orders, no minimum.
- **Enterprise customers:** Free expedited (2-day) shipping on all orders, no minimum.

Free shipping applies to the continental 48 states only. Alaska, Hawaii, and US territories
are excluded from free shipping offers.

### Processing Times

- Orders placed before 1 PM MT on a business day ship the same day.
- Orders placed after 1 PM MT or on weekends/holidays ship the next business day.
- Custom or backordered items may require additional processing time (noted on the product page).

## Alaska, Hawaii & US Territories

Shipping to Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, US Virgin Islands, and other territories is
available via USPS Priority Mail or FedEx.

| Destination | Standard Shipping Cost |
|---|---|
| Alaska / Hawaii | $15 flat rate |
| Puerto Rico | $12 flat rate |
| Other US Territories | $18 flat rate |

Free shipping thresholds do **not** apply to these destinations.

## International Shipping

International shipping is available to Canada, Mexico, the United Kingdom, and the European Union.
International orders are shipped via FedEx International Priority.

| Region | Estimated Delivery | Starting Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Canada | 3–7 business days | $20 |
| Mexico | 5–10 business days | $25 |
| UK | 5–10 business days | $35 |
| EU | 7–14 business days | $35 |

### International Customs & Duties

International customers are responsible for all import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees.
These charges are not included in our shipping rates and are collected by the destination country.
Acme Outfitters is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing.

### Restricted Items for International Shipping

- Fuel canisters and aerosols cannot be shipped internationally (IATA regulations).
- Sharp implements (axes, ice tools) may require special permits in some countries.

## Tracking

All orders include tracking. Tracking information is emailed when the shipping label is created.
You can also view tracking status in your account under Order History.

## Signature Requirements

Orders over $500 automatically require an adult signature at delivery. You may waive this
requirement during checkout at your own risk.

## Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you have not received your package:

1. Check with neighbors and any secure locations on your property.
2. Wait 24 hours — carriers occasionally mark packages delivered early.
3. Contact support@acmeoutfitters.example with your order number within 7 days.

We will file a claim with the carrier and either reship the order or issue a full refund.

## Address Changes

Address changes must be requested before the order ships. Contact support immediately after
placing an order if you need to update a shipping address. We cannot guarantee address changes
after a shipping label has been created.
