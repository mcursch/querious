# Shipping Policy — Acme Outfitters

## Overview

Acme Outfitters ships from three distribution centers: Denver, CO; Portland, OR; and Asheville, NC. Orders are routed to the nearest center with available stock. This document covers standard domestic shipping, expedited options, free-shipping thresholds, and international shipping rules.

## Carriers

We partner with the following carriers depending on package size, destination zone, and service level:

| Carrier | Used For |
|---|---|
| UPS Ground / UPS 2nd Day Air / UPS Next Day Air | Most domestic parcels |
| FedEx Ground / FedEx Home Delivery | Residential large-parcel deliveries |
| USPS Priority Mail | Small parcels under 1 lb, PO Box deliveries |
| LTL Freight (Estes, XPO) | Oversized items (large wall tents, kayaks, bulk enterprise orders) |

Carrier selection is made automatically at time of fulfillment based on destination and package weight. You cannot specify a preferred carrier on standard orders; enterprise account managers may negotiate preferred-carrier agreements.

## Domestic Shipping Options & Rates

| Service | Transit Time | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Ground | 5–7 business days | $8.95 flat, or free over threshold |
| 3-Day Select | 3 business days | $14.95 |
| 2-Day Air | 2 business days | $24.95 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $39.95 |

Transit times are estimates and begin on the next business day after shipment. Orders placed after **2:00 PM Mountain Time** on a business day ship the following business day. Orders placed on weekends or federal holidays ship the next business day.

## Free Shipping Threshold

Domestic US orders totaling **$75.00 or more** (after any discounts, before tax) qualify for **free Standard Ground shipping**. Expedited options are never free and always carry the rates shown above.

Premium and enterprise tier customers may have modified thresholds or complimentary expedited shipping as part of their tier benefits — see the Pricing Tiers document for details.

## Order Processing Time

Most in-stock orders are picked and shipped within **1 business day** of being placed. During peak season (November–January and May–June), processing times may extend to **2 business days**. You will receive a shipment confirmation email with a tracking number when your order leaves our warehouse.

## P.O. Boxes and Military Addresses

We ship to P.O. Boxes via USPS Priority Mail. We ship to APO/FPO/DPO military addresses via USPS; transit times to military addresses are not guaranteed and may exceed standard estimates.

## International Shipping

Acme Outfitters ships to **Canada and select Western European countries** (UK, Germany, France, Netherlands, Sweden, Norway, Austria, Switzerland). International shipping is not available for all products; climbing safety hardware is restricted to US/Canada only due to certification requirements.

| Region | Carrier | Estimated Transit | Shipping Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| Canada | UPS Standard International | 5–10 business days | Calculated at checkout |
| Western Europe | UPS Worldwide Saver | 7–14 business days | Calculated at checkout |

**Duties and taxes:** International customers are responsible for all import duties, taxes, and brokerage fees assessed by their country's customs authority. These charges are not included in the shipping rate shown at checkout and will be billed by the carrier upon delivery.

Acme Outfitters does not accept returns of international shipments refused at customs; the customer bears full responsibility for any returned-to-sender international shipment.

## Freight & Oversized Items

Items marked **"Oversized"** in the product listing ship via LTL freight and require a scheduled delivery appointment. Freight deliveries are curbside only — the carrier will not bring items inside. A valid phone number is required at checkout for all freight orders so the carrier can schedule delivery. Freight shipping rates are quoted at checkout based on destination zip code.

## Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking information shows "Delivered" but you have not received your package, please:

1. Check with neighbors and any building mailroom or front desk.
2. Wait 24 hours — carriers occasionally scan packages as delivered slightly early.
3. Contact Acme Support if the package does not appear within 24 hours of the "Delivered" scan.

We will file a carrier claim on your behalf. Resolution typically takes 5–10 business days. Acme Outfitters will reship or refund once the claim is resolved; we are not responsible for packages confirmed stolen after delivery (i.e., porch theft) but may offer a one-time courtesy credit at our discretion.
