# Shipping Policy

## Carriers

Acme Outfitters ships via UPS, FedEx, and USPS depending on package size, weight, and destination.

## Shipping Zones & Rates

| Zone | Standard (5–7 days) | Expedited (2–3 days) | Overnight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Continental US | $8.95 | $19.95 | $39.95 |
| Alaska/Hawaii | $14.95 | $34.95 | $59.95 |
| International | $24.95+ | Not available | Not available |

## Free Shipping Threshold

Orders of **$75 or more** (after discounts) ship free via standard shipping within the continental US.

## International Shipping

International orders ship to Canada and select European countries. Customers are responsible for import duties and taxes. Estimated delivery 10–21 business days.

## Order Processing

Orders placed before 2 PM Mountain Time ship the same business day. Orders after 2 PM ship the next business day.
