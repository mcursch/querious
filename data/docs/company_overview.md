# Acme Outfitters — Company Overview

## History

Acme Outfitters was founded in 1987 by siblings Dana and Marcus Webb in Portland, Oregon. What began as a single-room gear repair shop—re-stitching packs and resoling boots for local climbers—grew rapidly as the Pacific Northwest outdoor community expanded through the 1990s. In 1993 the Webbs opened a retail storefront, and by 1998 they had launched a mail-order catalog that reached customers in all 48 contiguous states.

The company made its first foray into e-commerce in 2001, and by 2008 online sales had surpassed catalog and in-store revenue combined. The Webb family retained majority ownership through two rounds of minority-stake private investment (2010, 2016) that funded distribution infrastructure expansion.

Today Acme Outfitters is a mid-market specialty outdoor retailer with approximately $180 million in annual revenue, serving recreational hikers, backpackers, climbers, and car-campers across North America.

## Mission and Values

### Mission Statement

*Equip every adventurer — beginner to expert — with gear and knowledge that makes the outdoors accessible, safe, and joyful.*

### Core Values

**Gear You Can Trust.** Acme Outfitters tests every product in real conditions before listing it. The in-house product team partners with field testers (guides, instructors, and long-distance hikers) to verify specs under realistic use.

**People First.** Whether it is an employee, a customer, or a supplier, Acme Outfitters builds relationships on transparency and respect. The company maintains a flat management structure and an open-door policy at every level.

**Environmental Stewardship.** Acme Outfitters commits 1% of annual gross revenue to trail-maintenance nonprofits and conservation organizations through the Leave It Better Fund, established in 2015. All outbound packaging used since 2021 is curbside-recyclable.

**Continuous Learning.** Employees are encouraged to get outside and use the products they sell. The company subsidizes up to $500 per employee per year in outdoor-activity expenses (guided trips, skills courses, race entries) through the Adventure Allowance program.

## Locations and Infrastructure

### Headquarters

Acme Outfitters' corporate headquarters is located at 4400 Cascade Boulevard, Portland, OR 97209. The HQ houses executive leadership, marketing, finance, HR, and the product and technology teams.

### Distribution Centers

**Portland DC** — 4420 Cascade Boulevard, Portland, OR 97209. Handles all west-coast and international shipments.

**Denver DC** — 18200 Frontier Industrial Pkwy, Denver, CO 80239. Handles Rocky Mountain and central US shipments.

**Nashville DC** — 7100 Distribution Drive, Nashville, TN 37209. Handles southeast and east-coast shipments. Opened in 2019 to cut average domestic delivery time by 1.4 days.

### Retail Stores

Acme Outfitters operates three company-owned retail stores (Portland OR, Denver CO, and Asheville NC) and partners with fifteen independent outfitters as authorized resellers.
