# Company Overview — Acme Outfitters

## Our Story

Acme Outfitters was founded in 1987 by siblings Dana and Jordan Mercer out of a small garage workshop in Boulder, Colorado. What started as a side project hand-stitching technical daypacks for local climbers quickly grew into a full-service outdoor gear retailer. By 1995 the company had outgrown its Boulder roots and moved into a 12,000-square-foot distribution center in Denver. Today Acme Outfitters employs more than 200 people across five states and ships to customers throughout North America and select international markets.

Our origin story is still very much part of who we are. Every Acme product is designed by people who actually use the gear — climbers, backpackers, cyclists, and kayakers — because we believe that authentic field experience produces better design decisions than a spreadsheet ever could.

## Mission & Values

**Mission:** To equip every adventurer — from the weekend car-camper to the alpine expedition team — with reliable, thoughtfully designed gear at a fair price.

**Core values:**

- **Integrity** — We stand behind everything we sell. If a product fails in normal use, we make it right.
- **Stewardship** — We take our impact on wild places seriously. Our Boulder headquarters is powered by 100% renewable energy, and 1% of annual revenue is donated to trail-maintenance nonprofits through our *Trails Forever* program.
- **Community** — We invest in the communities our customers explore, partnering with local guide services, youth outdoor programs, and public-land advocacy groups.
- **Quality over hype** — We do not chase trends. We iterate on proven designs until they are genuinely excellent before releasing them to market.

## Organizational Structure

Acme Outfitters is organized into five departments:

- **Sales** — Business development, account management, and enterprise partnerships. The Sales team manages relationships with our premium and enterprise tier customers and runs the annual catalog outreach cycle.
- **Support** — Customer experience, returns processing, warranty claims, and ticket escalation. Support staff work within the SLA commitments defined in our service-level agreement documentation.
- **Engineering** — Internal software systems, e-commerce platform, and data infrastructure. The Engineering team owns the internal tooling that powers the Querious assistant.
- **Finance** — Accounts receivable, accounts payable, payroll, and financial reporting. The Finance team produces quarterly business reviews shared with department heads.
- **Warehouse** — Inventory management, order picking and packing, shipping coordination, and receiving. Warehouse staff across our three distribution centers process thousands of shipments per week.

## Customer Tiers

Acme Outfitters serves three customer segments — standard, premium, and enterprise — each with different pricing, support, and service commitments. Full details are documented in the Pricing Tiers and SLA documents.
