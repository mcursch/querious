# Company Overview — Acme Outfitters

## About Us

Acme Outfitters was founded in 1987 in Denver, Colorado by two mountaineers — Sarah Chen and
Marcus Webb — who believed that quality outdoor gear should be accessible, durable, and backed
by genuine expertise. What started as a 1,500 sq ft retail shop near the base of the Front Range
has grown into a multi-channel retailer with over 200 employees serving customers across the US
and internationally.

Our mission: **Equip people to get outside and stay outside.**

## Customer Base

We serve three types of customers:

1. **Individual consumers** — recreational hikers, backpackers, climbers, skiers
2. **Small businesses / guides** — guide services, outfitters, outdoor education programs
3. **Enterprise / institutional** — REI co-op vendors, government agencies, search-and-rescue
   organizations

Customers are classified as Standard, Premium, or Enterprise (see `pricing_tiers.md`).

## Values

- **Gear that lasts:** We prioritize durability and repairability over disposability.
  Our Lifetime Repair Program reflects this commitment.
- **Know your gear:** Every customer-facing employee completes product training before
  helping customers. We don't sell gear we don't understand.
- **Leave it better:** We donate 1% of annual revenue to trail maintenance nonprofits through
  the 1% for the Planet program.
- **Radical honesty:** If a product isn't right for a customer's use case, we tell them.
