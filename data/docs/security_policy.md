# Security Policy

## Password Requirements

- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
- Rotate every 90 days
- No reuse of last 10 passwords
- MFA required for all company systems

## Data Handling

- Customer PII must never be stored in personal devices or unapproved cloud services
- Encrypt data at rest and in transit
- Report suspected breaches immediately to security@acmeoutfitters.example

## Incident Reporting

Report any security incident within 1 hour of discovery. Include: what happened, when, affected systems, and what action you took.
