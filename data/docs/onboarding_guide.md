# Acme Outfitters New-Hire Onboarding Guide

*People Operations — Updated January 2026*

---

## Overview

Welcome to Acme Outfitters! This guide walks you through everything you need to do during your
first week to get set up, connected, and ready to contribute. Your People Operations contact and
your assigned onboarding buddy will support you throughout this process.

**Your onboarding buddy** will be introduced via email before your start date. They are your
go-to resource for day-to-day questions that fall outside formal HR channels.

---

## Pre-Start Checklist (Before Day 1)

Complete these items as soon as you receive your offer-letter confirmation email:

- [ ] **Sign all offer and onboarding paperwork** via DocuSign (link emailed by People Ops).
- [ ] **Submit your I-9 identity documents** — bring originals to your first day or use the
  remote notary service linked in your onboarding email.
- [ ] **Choose your benefits elections** in the Benefitsly portal within 7 days of your start date
  (medical, dental, vision, 401(k) contribution rate).
- [ ] **Confirm your equipment delivery address** — see the Equipment section below.
- [ ] **Download and install Okta Verify** on your personal mobile phone; you'll need it for
  multi-factor authentication on Day 1.

---

## New-Hire Checklist — Week 1

### Systems Access

- [ ] **Activate your @acmeoutfitters.example Google Workspace account.**
  Log in at accounts.google.com with your company email. Your temporary password is in the
  welcome email from IT (it-helpdesk@acmeoutfitters.example).

- [ ] **Set up Okta Single Sign-On (SSO).**
  All internal tools (Slack, Jira, Confluence, Greenhouse, Expensify) are accessed through
  the Okta dashboard at okta.acmeoutfitters.example. Enroll your MFA device on Day 1.

- [ ] **Request role-specific system access.**
  Submit an IT Access Request ticket in Jira (project: IT-OPS) specifying your role and
  department. Standard provisioning SLA is 1 business day. Common systems by department:
  - *Sales*: Salesforce CRM, Gong
  - *Engineering*: GitHub org invite, AWS console (dev), PagerDuty
  - *Finance*: NetSuite, Expensify (admin tier)
  - *Support*: Zendesk, Looker
  - *Warehouse*: Fishbowl Inventory, ShipStation

- [ ] **Install and configure the company VPN (GlobalProtect).**
  Download the client from the IT portal (it.acmeoutfitters.example). VPN is required for
  all access to internal systems when off-site. See the Security Policy doc for full rules.

- [ ] **Join required Slack channels.**
  At minimum: `#general`, `#announcements`, `#your-team`, `#it-helpdesk`, and
  `#people-ops-updates`. Your onboarding buddy will invite you to team-specific channels.

### Equipment

- [ ] **Verify receipt of your standard equipment package.**
  All full-time hires receive:
  - 14" or 16" MacBook Pro (M-series) — configured by IT before shipping
  - USB-C hub with HDMI and USB-A ports
  - Wired USB keyboard and mouse
  - Acme Outfitters branded webcam

- [ ] **Request any additional peripherals within 5 business days of your start date.**
  Submit an Equipment Request in Jira (project: IT-OPS). Items above the standard package
  require manager approval. The remote office supply budget ($50/month, see Handbook §4.2)
  can be used for consumables and desk accessories via Expensify.

- [ ] **Register your equipment serial numbers in the IT asset portal.**
  Log in to it.acmeoutfitters.example → Asset Registry → Add Device. This is required for
  warranty claims and security compliance.

### First-Week Milestones

- [ ] **Complete mandatory compliance training modules** in the Acme Outfitters LMS
  (lms.acmeoutfitters.example). The following are required within your first 14 days:
  - *Information Security Awareness* (45 min)
  - *Anti-Harassment and Workplace Conduct* (30 min)
  - *Data Privacy Fundamentals* (20 min)
  Completion is tracked automatically; your manager is notified if modules are overdue.

- [ ] **Attend the New-Hire Orientation session.**
  Held every other Monday at 10:00 AM PT (hybrid). Your onboarding email includes a
  calendar invite. Agenda: company history, org structure, culture overview, benefits Q&A,
  IT security basics, and open Q&A with People Ops leadership.

- [ ] **Schedule a 1:1 with your direct manager by end of Day 2.**
  This meeting should cover: your 30/60/90-day goals, how you'll be measured, team
  communication norms, and any immediate priorities.

- [ ] **Schedule a 1:1 with your onboarding buddy by end of Day 3.**
  This is an informal "get to know the company" conversation — no agenda required.

- [ ] **Complete your employee profile in Workday.**
  Add your photo, pronouns (optional), office location or remote city, and emergency
  contact information. Workday is also where you'll submit PTO requests and view pay stubs.

- [ ] **Submit your first expense report if applicable.**
  If you incurred any pre-approved expenses (relocation, travel for orientation, etc.),
  submit them in Expensify within 30 days. See the Employee Handbook (§4) for limits and
  the receipt requirements.

- [ ] **Read the Employee Handbook.**
  Available at this same path in the RAG knowledge base and on the company intranet. Pay
  special attention to the Remote Work Policy (§3) and Code of Conduct (§5).

## 30 / 60 / 90 Day Goals

These are set collaboratively with your manager in your first 1:1, but here is the general
framework People Ops recommends:

| Milestone | Focus |
|---|---|
| 30 days | Learn — understand the team, tools, processes, and current projects |
| 60 days | Contribute — own at least one meaningful deliverable or project workstream |
| 90 days | Drive — operate independently; identify and propose one improvement |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**When do I get my first paycheck?**
Acme Outfitters pays bi-weekly (every other Friday). If your start date falls mid-cycle, you may
receive a partial first paycheck. Confirm your banking details in Workday on Day 1.

**How do I request PTO?**
Submit PTO requests in Workday at least 5 business days in advance for planned time off. For
urgent or unplanned leave, notify your manager directly as soon as possible. See the Employee
Handbook (§2) for accrual rates.

**What if my equipment doesn't arrive by Day 1?**
Contact IT Help Desk immediately. IT can provision a loaner device or set up temporary
browser-based access to critical tools while your primary equipment is in transit.

**Can I expense my home internet?**
No — home internet is not a reimbursable expense. The remote office supply budget ($50/month)
covers desk accessories and consumables only.
