# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Acme Outfitters

This guide covers everything you need to do in your first 30 days to get up and running.
Your manager and HR will walk through this with you during week 1, but keep this as a reference.

## Before Your First Day

HR will send you a welcome email with:
- Offer letter and I-9 instructions (complete before day 1)
- IT request form (complete so your accounts are ready)
- Benefits enrollment link (complete within 30 days of start date)
- Parking and building access instructions

## Day 1 Checklist

- [ ] Arrive at HQ (1420 Larimer St, Denver) or your assigned location by 9 AM
- [ ] Check in with front desk; receive temporary badge
- [ ] Meet your manager and team
- [ ] IT setup: laptop provisioning, accounts created (see Systems Access below)
- [ ] HR orientation with hr@acmeoutfitters.example (2 hours; covers benefits, policies, handbook)
- [ ] Read and sign the Employee Handbook acknowledgment form
- [ ] Complete required compliance trainings in LMS (2–3 hours)

## Week 1 Checklist

- [ ] 1:1 with manager to review 30/60/90 day expectations
- [ ] Introduction meetings with cross-functional partners
- [ ] Product training (see Product Knowledge below)
- [ ] Shadow a customer support call (Sales and Support teams)
- [ ] Attend your first team standup/meeting
- [ ] Set up your employee profile in Slack and the employee directory

### Passwords & Authentication

- All accounts use Google SSO where possible.
- MFA (authenticator app) is required on all accounts. Set up Google Authenticator or Authy.
- Password manager: we use 1Password for shared credentials. Request a license from IT.
- Never share passwords; do not store credentials in Slack, email, or documents.

within 30 days of start for customer-facing roles.

## Your First 30/60/90 Days

### Days 1–30 (Learn)
- Complete all onboarding checklist items above
- Achieve 80%+ on product knowledge quizzes
- Shadow experienced team members
- Identify 3 things you want to improve in your area

### Days 31–60 (Contribute)
- Begin taking on independent work in your role
- Build relationships with key cross-functional partners
- Provide feedback to manager on onboarding experience

### Days 61–90 (Deliver)
- Own your primary responsibilities independently
- Deliver a project or improvement initiative
- 90-day review with manager (formal check-in on performance and alignment)
