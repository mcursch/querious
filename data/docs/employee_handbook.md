# Employee Handbook — Acme Outfitters

*Last revised: January 2026. This handbook supersedes all prior versions. Questions about any policy should be directed to HR at hr@acmeoutfitters.example.*

---

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Outfitters. We believe that the best outdoor gear companies are staffed by people who love the outdoors. Whether you spend your weekends backpacking, climbing, paddling, or just sitting around a campfire, you are part of a team that shares your passion. This handbook outlines the policies, expectations, and benefits that govern employment at Acme Outfitters. Please read it carefully and keep it accessible for reference throughout your employment.

---

## 1. Employment Basics

### At-Will Employment

Employment at Acme Outfitters is at-will for all employees not covered by a specific written employment contract. Either party may end the employment relationship at any time for any lawful reason.

### Work Hours

Standard full-time employees are expected to work **40 hours per week**. Core business hours are **9:00 AM – 3:00 PM Mountain Time**, Monday through Friday. Employees may flex their start and end times outside this core window with manager approval, provided they meet their weekly hour commitment and are available during core hours for meetings and collaboration.

Part-time employees work hours as specified in their offer letter or a subsequent written agreement with their manager.

### Overtime

Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay (1.5× regular rate) for hours worked over 40 in a week. Overtime must be approved in advance by a manager. Working unapproved overtime does not waive the right to overtime pay, but employees who work unapproved overtime may be subject to corrective action.

## 2. Remote Work Policy

Acme Outfitters supports a **hybrid work model**. All employees in non-Warehouse roles are eligible to work remotely for **up to 2 days per week** after successfully completing their 90-day onboarding period.

### Eligibility Requirements

- Employee must have passed the 90-day onboarding period with a satisfactory performance assessment.
- Employee must maintain a dedicated, ergonomic home work environment.
- Employee must be reachable by phone, chat, and video during core business hours (9:00 AM – 3:00 PM MT) on remote days.
- Employees with active performance improvement plans (PIPs) are not eligible for remote work until the PIP is resolved.

### Warehouse & Fulfillment Staff

Warehouse and fulfillment roles are on-site only. The hybrid policy does not apply to these positions due to the hands-on nature of inventory, receiving, and shipping operations.

### Extended Remote Work

Extended remote work arrangements (more than 2 days per week or fully remote) require VP-level approval and are documented in writing. These are granted case-by-case and are not guaranteed.

## 3. Paid Time Off (PTO)

Acme Outfitters uses a unified PTO bank that covers vacation, personal time, and routine sick days.

### Accrual Schedule

| Employment Status | Annual PTO Accrual |
|---|---|
| Years 0–2 (full-time) | **15 days per year** |
| Years 3–5 (full-time) | **18 days per year** |
| Years 6+ (full-time) | **22 days per year** |
| Part-time employees | Pro-rated based on scheduled hours |

PTO accrues semi-monthly (on the 1st and 15th). New employees begin accruing PTO on their first day of employment.

### PTO Usage

- PTO requests must be submitted in the HR system at least **5 business days in advance** for requests of 3 or fewer consecutive days.
- Requests for 4 or more consecutive days must be submitted **at least 2 weeks in advance**.
- Approval is at manager discretion. Requests during peak season (November–January) require earlier notice and may be limited.

### Sick Leave

Routine sick days (illness, medical appointments) come from the general PTO bank. Extended illness of more than 5 consecutive days may qualify for short-term disability leave; contact HR for details.

### PTO Carryover

Employees may carry over **a maximum of 10 days of PTO** into the next calendar year. PTO in excess of 10 days as of December 31 is forfeited. Acme does not pay out unused PTO above the carryover limit.

### PTO Payout at Separation

Upon voluntary resignation with at least 2 weeks' notice, or upon involuntary separation without cause, accrued and unused PTO up to the carryover limit (10 days) will be paid out in the final paycheck.

## 4. Expense Reimbursement

Acme reimburses pre-approved business expenses. All expense claims must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense being incurred.

### Approval Thresholds

| Amount | Required Approval |
|---|---|
| Under $50 | No receipt required; manager approval at claim time |
| $50 – $500 | Receipt required; manager approval |
| $501 – $2,000 | Receipt required; manager + department VP approval |
| Over $2,000 | Receipt required; VP + CFO approval |

### Common Reimbursable Expenses

- Travel (flights, hotels, ground transport) for pre-approved business trips
- Client entertainment (meals, events) — must note attendees and business purpose
- Home office equipment (one-time allowance of $400 for fully-remote employees)
- Training, conference registrations, and professional memberships — manager approval required

### Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Personal meals during regular office days
- Alcohol over $25 per person at business entertainment events
- Fines, parking tickets, or traffic violations
- Expenses not pre-approved by manager (except emergency travel under $200)

## 5. Code of Conduct

Acme Outfitters is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and safe workplace. All employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, vendors, and the public with professionalism and respect.
- Avoid conflicts of interest; disclose any outside business activities that may conflict with your Acme responsibilities to HR.
- Protect confidential company and customer information (see the Security Policy for specifics).
- Report suspected violations of law, company policy, or this code to HR or via the anonymous ethics hotline at ethics.acmeoutfitters.example.

Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation are strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

---

## 6. Benefits Summary

- **Health insurance:** Medical, dental, and vision plans available; employee premiums range from $0 (employee-only) to $220/month (family) depending on plan selected.
- **401(k):** Acme matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of annual salary, vesting immediately.
- **Gear discount:** All employees receive **30% off** Acme products for personal use, with an annual spending cap of $1,500. Gear may not be purchased for resale.
- **Professional development:** Up to $1,000 per year in tuition or training reimbursement with manager approval.
- **Parental leave:** 8 weeks paid parental leave for birth/adoption, available to all parents.
