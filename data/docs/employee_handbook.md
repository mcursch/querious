# Employee Handbook

## Welcome to Acme Outfitters

This handbook outlines company policies, benefits, and expectations for all Acme Outfitters employees.

## PTO Policy

Full-time employees accrue PTO as follows:

| Years of Service | PTO Accrual |
|---|---|
| 0–1 years | 10 days/year |
| 1–3 years | 15 days/year |
| 3–5 years | 18 days/year |
| 5+ years | 20 days/year |

Unused PTO may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days. PTO is not paid out at termination unless required by state law.

## Remote Work Policy

- Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week** with manager approval.
- Core hours: 10 AM–3 PM Mountain Time (all time zones must be available).
- Remote work arrangements may be revoked if performance suffers.

## Expense Policy

| Category | Limit |
|---|---|
| Meals (solo) | $50/day |
| Meals (client entertainment) | $150/person |
| Hotel | $200/night |
| Flight | Economy class only |
| Equipment | Up to $500 without pre-approval |

All expenses require receipts and manager approval within 30 days.

## Code of Conduct

Acme Outfitters is committed to a respectful, inclusive workplace. Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind will not be tolerated. Report concerns to HR at hr@acmeoutfitters.example.

## Benefits

- Health, dental, and vision insurance (employee + family coverage available)
- 401(k) with 4% company match, vested immediately
- Annual gear stipend: $500 for outdoor equipment purchases
- Employee discount: 30% on all Acme Outfitters products
