# Employee Handbook — Acme Outfitters

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Outfitters! This handbook outlines the policies and expectations for all
employees. It is your responsibility to read, understand, and follow these guidelines.
Please direct questions to hr@acmeoutfitters.example or your direct manager.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:

- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism.
- Maintain confidentiality of customer data, pricing, and business strategy.
- Avoid conflicts of interest; disclose any outside employment that may conflict with your role.
- Not use company resources (systems, email, credit cards) for personal purposes.
- Report any known or suspected violations of law or company policy to HR or your manager.

Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including
termination.

## Work Hours & Remote Work

### Standard Hours

Office-based roles: Monday–Friday, 8 AM–5 PM local time (core hours 10 AM–3 PM MT).
Warehouse roles: Shift schedule assigned by Warehouse Manager.

### Remote Work Policy

| Department | Remote Eligibility |
|---|---|
| Engineering | Up to 4 days remote per week |
| Finance | Up to 3 days remote per week |
| Sales | Up to 3 days remote per week (on-site for team meetings) |
| Support | Up to 2 days remote per week |
| Warehouse | On-site required (role-based exception requires VP approval) |

New employees in their first 90 days are expected to be on-site at least 3 days per week
regardless of department, to facilitate onboarding and relationship building.

Remote work privileges may be revoked for performance reasons with 2 weeks' notice.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Acme Outfitters uses a unified PTO policy (vacation, sick, personal days combined).

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual |
|---|---|
| 0–1 years | 10 days (accrued monthly) |
| 1–3 years | 15 days (accrued monthly) |
| 3–5 years | 18 days (accrued monthly) |
| 5+ years | 22 days (accrued monthly) |

### PTO Rules

- **Rollover:** Up to 5 unused PTO days may roll over into the next calendar year.
- **Payout:** PTO is not paid out upon voluntary resignation. It is paid out upon layoff or
  involuntary termination per state law.
- **Advance requests:** Requests of 3+ consecutive days require 2 weeks' advance notice.
- **Blackout periods:** Q4 (October–December) is a restricted period for Warehouse staff
  due to peak season. PTO requests during this window require manager and VP approval.

### Holidays

Acme Outfitters observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Juneteenth
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Day

When a holiday falls on a weekend, the adjacent Friday or Monday is observed.

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees are reimbursed for reasonable, documented business expenses.

### Limits (no prior approval required)

| Category | Limit |
|---|---|
| Meals (solo) | $30 per meal |
| Meals (client entertainment) | $75 per person |
| Ground transportation (taxi/rideshare) | $50 per trip |
| Hotel | $200/night (tier 1 cities: $275/night) |
| Airfare | Lowest available economy fare |
| Conference registration | $500 (above requires VP approval) |

Expenses above these limits require manager pre-approval. All expenses require receipts.

Submit expenses within 30 days of incurring them via the Expensify app. Expenses submitted
more than 90 days late will not be reimbursed.

## Benefits

- **Health insurance:** Medical, dental, and vision via Anthem. Employer covers 80% of employee
  premium; 50% for dependents.
- **401(k):** 4% company match, vests immediately.
- **Life insurance:** 1x annual salary, employer-paid.
- **Employee discount:** 30% off all Acme Outfitters products.
- **Gear lending library:** Employees may borrow gear from the warehouse for personal trips
  (available upon request, limit 1 active loan per employee).

## Performance Reviews

- New employees: 30-day check-in, 90-day formal review.
- All employees: Annual performance review in November; mid-year check-in in May.
- Compensation adjustments are made following the annual review, effective January 1.

## Conduct & Disciplinary Policy

Minor violations may result in a verbal warning, written warning, or performance improvement
plan (PIP). Serious violations (theft, harassment, safety violations, data breaches) may
result in immediate termination.

Employees have the right to appeal disciplinary actions by contacting HR within 10 business
days of receiving written notice.
