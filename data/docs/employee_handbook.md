*Last revised: January 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Acme Outfitters. This handbook describes the policies and expectations that govern your
employment. Please read it carefully and reach out to People Operations with any questions.

## 2. PTO and Leave

### 2.1 PTO Accrual

All full-time employees accrue Paid Time Off (PTO) based on years of service:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual |
|---|---|
| 0–1 year | 10 days per year (0.83 days per pay period) |
| 1–3 years | 15 days per year (1.25 days per pay period) |
| 3–7 years | 20 days per year (1.67 days per pay period) |
| 7+ years | 25 days per year (2.08 days per pay period) |

- PTO accrues each bi-weekly pay period beginning on your first day of employment.
- Unused PTO may be carried over up to a maximum of **40 hours (5 days)** at calendar year end.
- Accrued but unused PTO is paid out upon separation from the company.

### 2.2 Sick Leave

Employees receive **5 days (40 hours)** of separate sick leave per calendar year, granted in full
on January 1 (or on the first day of employment for new hires). Sick leave does not carry over and
is not paid out upon separation.

### 2.3 Holidays

Acme Outfitters observes **11 paid holidays** per year:

1. New Year's Day
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day
3. Presidents' Day
4. Memorial Day
5. Juneteenth
6. Independence Day
7. Labor Day
8. Thanksgiving Day
9. Day after Thanksgiving
10. Christmas Eve (half day)
11. Christmas Day

### 2.4 Other Leave

- **Bereavement**: Up to 5 days of paid leave for the death of an immediate family member; up to 3
  days for extended family.
- **Jury Duty**: Paid for up to 15 business days; employees must provide a court summons to People
  Operations.
- **Parental Leave**: 12 weeks of paid parental leave (birth, adoption, or foster placement),
  available after 6 months of employment.

## 3. Remote Work Policy

### 3.1 Eligibility

Remote work is available to employees in roles designated as "remote-eligible" by their department
head. Warehouse and in-store roles are not eligible for remote work.

### 3.2 Core Hours

All remote employees must be available and responsive during **core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in
their primary time zone**, Monday through Friday.

### 3.3 Equipment and Workspace

- Acme Outfitters provides a standard remote equipment package (see the Onboarding Guide for
  details on requesting equipment).
- Employees are responsible for maintaining a quiet, professional workspace for video calls.
- A reliable internet connection (minimum **25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload**) is required.

### 3.4 In-Office Requirements

Remote employees must travel to the nearest Acme Outfitters office for:
- Quarterly all-hands meetings (4 times per year).
- New-employee orientation (first week on-site).
- Any meetings explicitly designated as in-person by a director or above.

Travel costs for required in-office visits are reimbursed under the expense policy below.

### 3.5 Security Requirements for Remote Work

- All work must be performed over the company VPN when accessing internal systems.
- Public or shared Wi-Fi networks require VPN use at all times.
- Physical documents containing confidential information must not be left unattended.

## 4. Expense Reimbursement

### 4.1 Reimbursable Expenses

Acme Outfitters reimburses reasonable and necessary business expenses, including:
- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Client meals and entertainment
- Required office supplies for remote workers
- Professional development courses and certifications (pre-approval required)

### 4.2 Reimbursement Caps

| Category | Limit |
|---|---|
| Meals (per diem, domestic) | **$75 per day** |
| Hotel (domestic, per night) | **$250 per night** |
| Airfare | Coach class only; business class requires VP approval |
| Remote office supplies (per month) | **$50 per month** |
| Professional development (per year) | **$1,500 per calendar year** |
| Client entertainment (single event) | **$150 per person in attendance** |

All expenses above **$25** require an itemized receipt. Expenses above **$500** require manager
pre-approval before they are incurred.

### 4.3 Submission Deadline

Expense reports must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense date. Reports submitted after
60 days may be denied.

### 4.4 Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not reimbursable under any circumstances:
- Personal meals when not traveling for business
- Traffic or parking fines
- Alcohol (except reasonable amounts during approved client entertainment)
- Personal subscriptions or memberships
- First-class or business-class airfare without prior VP approval

## 5. Code of Conduct

### 5.1 Core Principles

All Acme Outfitters employees are expected to:
- Act with **integrity** in every interaction, internal and external.
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with **respect and professionalism**.
- Protect company and customer **confidential information**.
- Comply with all **applicable laws and regulations**.
- Report known or suspected violations without fear of retaliation.

### 5.2 Anti-Harassment and Non-Discrimination

Acme Outfitters is committed to a workplace free from harassment and discrimination on the basis of
race, color, religion, sex, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation,
or any other characteristic protected by law.

Harassment includes, but is not limited to:
- Unwelcome sexual advances or comments
- Offensive jokes, slurs, or derogatory language
- Intimidation, bullying, or threatening behavior
- Displaying offensive images or materials

**Reporting**: Report incidents to your manager, any member of the People Operations team, or via
the anonymous Ethics Hotline (details provided separately). All reports are investigated promptly
and confidentially.

### 5.3 Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any situation where personal interests could conflict or appear to conflict
with Acme Outfitters' interests. Common examples:
- Outside employment with a competitor or supplier
- Personal financial interest in a vendor the company does business with
- Hiring or supervising a close family member

Disclosures should be made in writing to People Operations.

### 5.4 Confidentiality

Employees must not disclose trade secrets, proprietary business information, customer data, or
personnel information to unauthorized parties, during or after employment.

### 5.5 Social Media

- Do not share confidential company information on personal social media accounts.
- Clearly distinguish personal opinions from official Acme Outfitters positions.
- When in doubt, consult the Communications team before posting about company matters.

### 5.6 Disciplinary Action

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including
termination of employment, and may be referred to law enforcement where appropriate.
