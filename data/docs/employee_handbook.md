# Acme Outfitters Employee Handbook

*Effective January 1, 2025. Supersedes all prior versions.*

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Outfitters. This handbook describes the policies, benefits, and expectations that govern life at the company. It is not an employment contract; Acme Outfitters is an at-will employer. When policies change, HR will distribute an updated version and post it to the internal wiki.

Questions about anything in this document should go to hr@acmeoutfitters.example or your department manager.

---

## Time Off and Leave

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees accrue PTO from their first day. Accrual rates by tenure:

| Years of Service | Annual PTO Accrual |
|------------------|--------------------|
| 0–1 year | 12 days |
| 2–4 years | 17 days |
| 5–9 years | 22 days |
| 10+ years | 27 days |

Part-time employees (20–32 hours/week) accrue PTO at 50% of the rates above. PTO may be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days into the new calendar year; any balance above 10 days is forfeited on December 31.

### Requesting Time Off

Time-off requests must be submitted in Acme's HR portal (Rippling) at least **five business days** in advance for requests of one to three days, and at least **three weeks** in advance for requests of four or more consecutive days. Requests are subject to manager approval. Holiday blackout windows apply to Warehouse and Support teams during November 15 – December 31.

### Sick Leave

Employees accrue one hour of sick leave for every 30 hours worked, up to a maximum of 72 hours per calendar year. Sick leave does not roll over. Employees who are ill for more than three consecutive days may be required to provide a medical note.

### Company Holidays

Acme Outfitters observes 11 paid holidays per year:

1. New Year's Day
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day
3. Presidents' Day
4. Memorial Day
5. Juneteenth
6. Independence Day
7. Labor Day
8. Indigenous Peoples' Day
9. Thanksgiving Day
10. Day after Thanksgiving
11. Christmas Day

When a holiday falls on a Saturday, the preceding Friday is observed. When it falls on a Sunday, the following Monday is observed.

### Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks fully paid. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event.

## Remote Work

### Eligibility

Roles designated as **Remote-Eligible** in the job description may work remotely full-time, subject to manager approval and a signed remote-work agreement. Roles classified as **Hybrid** require a minimum of two days per week on-site at an Acme office or DC. Roles classified as **On-Site** (most Warehouse positions) require full in-person attendance.

### Remote Work Requirements

Remote employees must:
- Maintain a private, distraction-limited workspace during core hours (10 AM – 3 PM in their local time zone).
- Have a reliable internet connection capable of at least 25 Mbps download.
- Use Acme-issued or Acme-approved hardware. Personal devices used for work must have Jamf MDM enrolled.
- Join all-hands and team meetings via video with camera on unless a reasonable accommodation has been approved.

### International Remote Work

Employees may not work remotely from outside the United States for more than **10 consecutive calendar days** per year without advance written approval from HR and their VP. Work from outside the US may create tax nexus or employment-law obligations that require advance planning.

## Expenses and Reimbursement

### Expense Policy

Acme reimburses reasonable, pre-approved business expenses. Submit receipts via Expensify within **30 days** of incurring the expense. Expenses submitted more than 60 days after the transaction date will not be reimbursed.

### Per-Diem and Travel Limits

| Category | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Meals (per day, domestic travel) | $75 |
| Hotel (per night, tier-1 city) | $225 |
| Hotel (per night, other domestic) | $175 |
| Ground transport (ride-share per day) | $50 |
| Economy airfare | Book via Navan; no personal booking |

Business class airfare requires VP approval and is only authorized for flights over six hours.

### Equipment Purchases

Individual purchases of work equipment (monitors, peripherals, desk furniture) require pre-approval from your manager and Finance. The standard new-employee equipment budget is $1,500. Subsequent equipment requests above $300 require a ticket in Jira Service Desk.

### Adventure Allowance

Each full-time employee receives up to **$500 per calendar year** to spend on outdoor activities—guided trips, skills courses, gear clinics, trail-running or climbing events—reimbursable through Expensify with the category tag "Adventure Allowance." Unused allowance does not roll over.

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior

Acme Outfitters expects all employees to act with integrity and treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or retaliation of any kind is prohibited. This applies in the office, at company events, and in digital communication channels.

### Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose to HR any outside employment, financial interest, or personal relationship that could conflict with their role at Acme. Situations requiring disclosure include: owning equity in a competitor or supplier, having a close personal relationship with a direct report, and working as a consultant for an Acme vendor.

### Confidentiality

Information about customers, pricing, finances, product roadmaps, and employee compensation is confidential. Do not share internal information on social media or with third parties outside the scope of your job duties. Confidentiality obligations survive employment.

### Social Media

Employees may reference their employment at Acme Outfitters on personal social media but must not post confidential information, speak on behalf of the company without authorization from Marketing, or disparage customers, colleagues, or competitors.

### Reporting Concerns

Concerns about policy violations, unethical conduct, or safety issues can be reported to your manager, HR, or anonymously through the Ethics Hotline (ethicshotline.acmeoutfitters.example). Acme prohibits retaliation against good-faith reporters.

## Performance and Development

### Review Cycle

Performance reviews are conducted annually in January for the prior calendar year. Mid-year check-ins occur in July. Managers provide written feedback; employees complete a self-assessment in Rippling before each review cycle.

### Learning and Development Budget

All full-time employees receive **$1,000 per year** for professional development: conferences, online courses, certifications, or books. Submit requests in advance via the L&D form in the internal wiki. Manager approval is required for amounts over $500.

---

## Workplace Safety

Employees who identify a safety hazard—faulty equipment, blocked exits, unsafe conditions in the warehouse—must report it immediately to their manager and to safety@acmeoutfitters.example. Do not attempt to correct electrical or structural hazards yourself; file a report and wait for Facilities.

All warehouse employees are required to complete the OSHA 10-hour General Industry training within 60 days of hire.
