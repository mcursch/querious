# Product Guide: Stoves

## Stove Line Overview

### CanisterLite
Compact canister stove, compatible with standard isobutane-propane canisters.
- Boil time: 3.5 min/liter
- Fuel: Isobutane-propane (not included)

### WhisperFlame
Wind-resistant canister stove with radiant burner.
- Boil time: 4 min/liter

### LiquidFuel Pro
Multi-fuel stove for cold-weather and international travel.
- Fuel: White gas, kerosene, unleaded gasoline

## Safety Notes

Never operate stoves indoors or in enclosed spaces. Keep fuel canisters away from heat. Inspect O-rings before each use.
