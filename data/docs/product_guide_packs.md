# Product Guide: Backpacks

## Pack Line Overview

### DayHiker Series (15–30L)
Frameless daypacks for hiking and travel.

### TrailRunner Series (30–50L)
Internal frame packs for weekend trips.

### Expedition Series (55–85L)
Full-featured expedition packs with hip-belt pockets and sleeping bag compartment.

## Fit Guide

Measure your torso length (C7 vertebra to top of hip shelf). Use the fit chart in-store or online to select the correct frame size.
